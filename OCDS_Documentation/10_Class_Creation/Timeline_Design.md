## ðŸŽ¯ Timeline Principles

### 1. Logical Progression

Build concepts sequentially:
```
Week 1: Fundamentals
Week 2: Build on Week 1
Week 3: Apply Weeks 1-2
Week 4: Integrate all concepts
```

---

### 2. Balanced Workload

**Recommended per week:**
- Study material: 45-60 minutes
- Flashcards: 20-30 minutes
- Quiz: 15-20 minutes
- Homework: 60-90 minutes
- **Total:** 2.5-3.5 hours

---

### 3. Strategic Spacing

- Space similar topics across weeks
- Allow time for concept integration
- Build in review weeks
- Plan for midterm/final assessments

## ðŸŽ¯ Material Types

### Study Materials (Core Content)

**Purpose:** Deliver primary teaching content

**Best for:**
- Conceptual explanations
- Detailed information
- Reference material

**Guidelines:**
- 1,000-2,000 words per week
- Clear headings and structure
- Include examples and diagrams
- Link to related concepts

---

### Flashcards (Retention)

**Purpose:** Reinforce key terms and concepts

**Best for:**
- Terminology
- Quick facts
- Pattern recognition

**Guidelines:**
- 15-25 cards per week
- One concept per card
- Clear, concise questions
- Include context in answers

---

### Quizzes (Assessment)

**Purpose:** Test knowledge and understanding

**Best for:**
- Knowledge recall
- Concept application
- Progress checkpoints

**Guidelines:**
- 10-15 questions per week
- Mix difficulty levels
- Cover all key topics
- Provide explanations

---

### Homework (Application)

**Purpose:** Apply knowledge to real scenarios

**Best for:**
- Case studies
- Analysis tasks
- Creative projects

**Guidelines:**
- 1-2 assignments per week
- Clear rubrics
- Reasonable time commitment (1-2 hours)
- Meaningful feedback

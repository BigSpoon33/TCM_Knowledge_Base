### 1. Information Overload

**Problem:** Too much content per week

**Solution:**
- Limit to 1-2 hours of reading
- Focus on key concepts
- Save details for optional resources

---

### 2. Unclear Instructions

**Problem:** Students don't know what to do

**Solution:**
- Step-by-step instructions
- Clear expectations
- Examples provided

---

### 3. Inconsistent Difficulty

**Problem:** Week 2 harder than Week 10

**Solution:**
- Progressive difficulty
- Test with sample students
- Adjust based on feedback

---

### 4. Poor Quiz Questions

**Problem:** Ambiguous or trick questions

**Solution:**
- One clear correct answer
- Plausible distractors
- Test questions yourself

---

### 5. Unrealistic Workload

**Problem:** Students overwhelmed

**Solution:**
- Track time estimates
- Get feedback
- Adjust as needed

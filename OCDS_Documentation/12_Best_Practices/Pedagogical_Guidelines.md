### 1. Clear Learning Objectives

State what students will learn:
- Use action verbs (identify, analyze, apply)
- Be specific and measurable
- Align assessments with objectives

---

### 2. Chunked Content

Break information into digestible pieces:
- 10-15 minute reading segments
- One concept per section
- Frequent checkpoints

---

### 3. Active Learning

Engage students with:
- Practice questions
- Case studies
- Reflection prompts
- Discussion forums

---

### 4. Timely Feedback

Provide feedback that:
- Is specific and actionable
- Arrives within 1 week
- Encourages improvement
- Recognizes strengths

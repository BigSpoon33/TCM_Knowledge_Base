# **Da Huang (大黃)** · *Rhei Radix et Rhizoma* · rhubarb root and rhizome
## What is the standard Dosage for this herb?
?
**Dosage:** 3-15g internally. For a strong purgative effect, either add near the end or boil separately for 3 minutes and then add the resulting liquid to the strained decoction of the other ingredients.

# **Lu Hui (芦荟)** · *Aloe* · dried concentrate of the juice of the aloe leaf
## What is the standard Dosage for this herb?
?
**Dosage:** 1.5-4.5g. Used in pills or capsules; do not decoct.

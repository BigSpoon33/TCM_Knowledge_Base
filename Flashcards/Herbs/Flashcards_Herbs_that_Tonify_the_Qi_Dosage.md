?

# **Ren Shen (人参)** · *Ginseng Radix* · Ginseng Root
## What is the standard Dosage for this herb?
?
**Dosage:** 3-9 g. When taken directly as a powder, the dosage is 0.5-1g. In emergencies up to 30g can be used, divided into multiple doses.
<!--SR:!2025-11-10,3,250-->

# **Shān Yào (山药)** · *Dioscoreae Rhizoma* · Dioscorea rhizome, Chinese yam
## What is the standard Dosage for this herb?
?
**Dosage:** 9-30g; when a large dosage is required, from 60-240g may be used.

# **Yí Táng (饴糖)** · *Maltosum* · Malt sugar, maltose
## What is the standard Dosage for this herb?
?
**Dosage:** 15-60g. Do not cook in decoctions, but take with the strained decoction.

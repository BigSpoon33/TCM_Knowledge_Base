# **Ma Zi Ren Wan** (Cannabis Fruit Pill, Hemp Seed Pill, Ma Ren Wan)

- Constipation in elderly and debilitated patients

- Post-operative constipation

- Irritable bowel syndrome with constipation (IBS-C)

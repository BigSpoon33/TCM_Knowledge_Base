# **Qing Zao Jiu Fei Tang** (Clear Dryness and Rescue the Lungs Decoction, Autumn Dryness Formula, Qingzao Jiufei Tang)
## What **Diseases and Western Conditions** is this formula used for?
?
**TCM Diseases:**
- Chemotherapy-induced lung dryness
- Chronic pharyngitis and laryngitis
- Occupational lung exposure to dry environments

**Western Conditions:**
- Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) with dryness
- Interstitial lung disease with dry cough
- Post-viral cough syndrome

# **Xing Su San** (Apricot Kernel and Perilla Powder, Xingsu San)
## What **Diseases and Western Conditions** is this formula used for?
?
**TCM Diseases:**
- Acute bronchitis and pneumonia (early stage)
- Allergic asthma with cold triggers
- Chronic rhinosinusitis
- Upper respiratory tract infections

**Western Conditions:**
- Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) exacerbations
- Post-viral cough syndrome

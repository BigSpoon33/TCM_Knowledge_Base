# **Ban Xia Xie Xin Tang** (Pinellia Decoction to Drain the Epigastrium, Half-Summer Heart-Draining Decoction, Banxia Xiexin Tang)

?

- Chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting
- Chronic gastritis and gastroenteritis
- Post-operative digestive recovery

**Western Conditions:**
- Functional gastrointestinal disorders
- Gastroesophageal reflux disease
- Irritable bowel syndrome

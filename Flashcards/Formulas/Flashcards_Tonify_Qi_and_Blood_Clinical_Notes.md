# **Jia Wei Gui Pi Tang** (Augmented Restore the Spleen Decoction, Modified Gui Pi Tang, Enhanced Spleen-Restoring Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Jia Wei" means "added flavors" - referring to the addition of Chai Hu and Shan Zhi to Gui Pi Tang. This modification addresses the common clinical scenario where heart-spleen deficiency occurs with liver qi stagnation. The liver component is essential for people with stress-related insomnia and mood issues.

# **Zeng Ye Cheng Qi Tang** (Increase the Fluids and Order the Qi Decoction, Fluid-Increasing Order Qi Decoction, Zengye Chengqi Tang)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses once or twice daily as needed
**Preparation**: Mang Xiao dissolved in strained decoction, standard cooking for others
**Timing**: Usually taken in evening for morning bowel movement
**Duration**: Short-term for acute constipation, longer with modifications for chronic patterns

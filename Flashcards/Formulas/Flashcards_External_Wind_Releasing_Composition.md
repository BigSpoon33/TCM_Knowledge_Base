# **Xiao Feng San** (Eliminate Wind Powder, Dispel Wind Powder, Xiaofeng San)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, Shi Gao cooked first
**Timing**: Between meals for better absorption
**Duration**: 6-12 weeks for chronic skin conditions, longer for severe cases

# **Huo Xiang Zheng Qi San** (Agastache Powder to Rectify the Qi, Patchouli Qi-Correcting Powder, Koshosan)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules 2-3 times daily
**Preparation**: Light decoction to preserve volatile oils, add aromatic herbs last
**Timing**: Every 4-6 hours during acute phase, then twice daily
**Duration**: 3-7 days for acute conditions, 2-4 weeks for chronic digestive issues

# **Huan Shao Dan** (Rejuvenating Pill, Return to Youth Pill, Restoration Elixir)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, can be made into pills for long-term use
**Timing**: Morning and evening doses, morning dose may be more energizing
**Duration**: 3-6 months minimum for constitutional effects, often used long-term

# **You Gui Wan** (Right-Restoring Pill, Restore the Right Kidney Pill, You Gui Pills)

?

**Preparation**: Usually taken as pills, can be decocted
**Timing**: Between meals or with warm water
**Duration**: 3-6 months minimum for chronic conditions

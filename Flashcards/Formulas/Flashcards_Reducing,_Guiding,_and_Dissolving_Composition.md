# **Bao He Wan** (Preserve and Harmonize Pills, Citrus and Crataegus Formula, Food Stagnation Formula)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Can be taken as pills, decoction, or powder
**Timing**: After meals for acute indigestion, before meals for chronic issues
**Duration**: 3-7 days for acute symptoms, 2-4 weeks for chronic conditions

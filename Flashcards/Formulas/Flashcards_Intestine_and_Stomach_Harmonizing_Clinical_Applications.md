# **Ban Xia Xie Xin Tang** (Pinellia Decoction to Drain the Epigastrium, Half-Summer Heart-Draining Decoction, Banxia Xiexin Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Stomach disharmony with cold-heat complex and phlegm obstruction
**Key Symptoms**: Nausea, vomiting, epigastric fullness and pain, diarrhea, borborygmus, loss of appetite, alternating hot and cold sensations, restlessness
**Tongue**: Yellow greasy coating or red center with white edges
**Pulse**: Rapid or wiry and slippery

# **Gui Lu Er Xian Jiao** (Two-Immortal Decoction with Tortoise Shell and Deer Antler, Turtle and Deer Two Immortals Glue, Keirokunisenkyou)

**Pattern**: Kidney yin and yang deficiency with reproductive dysfunction
**Key Symptoms**: Menopausal symptoms, hot flashes with cold limbs, irregular menstruation, infertility, impotence, night sweats, fatigue, lower back pain, memory problems

?

**Pattern**: Kidney essence deficiency with blood deficiency affecting hair and aging
**Key Symptoms**: Premature graying, hair loss, thinning hair, brittle nails, fatigue, lower back pain, knee weakness, poor memory, early aging signs, dizziness

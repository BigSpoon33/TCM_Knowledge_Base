# **Er Chen Tang** (Two-Cured Decoction, Citrus and Pinellia Combination)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, simmer Ban Xia longer to reduce toxicity
**Timing**: Between meals for digestive symptoms, any time for respiratory symptoms
**Duration**: 2-6 weeks for acute conditions, longer for chronic phlegm patterns

# **Yin Qiao San** (Honeysuckle and Forsythia Powder, Lonicera and Forsythia Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Exterior wind-heat with beginning heat toxicity
**Key Symptoms**: Fever higher than chills, slight sweating, headache, thirst, sore throat, cough, restlessness
**Tongue**: Tip red, thin white or yellow coating
**Pulse**: Floating and rapid

# **Zhi Sou San** (Stop Cough Powder, Antitussive Powder, Zhisou San)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: External wind with internal phlegm causing chronic cough
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic cough with white or yellow phlegm, throat itching, chest tightness, slight chills, mild fever or no fever, fatigue
**Tongue**: Normal or slightly red with white or thin yellow coating
**Pulse**: Floating or normal, may be slightly rapid

# **Da Cheng Qi Tang** (Major Order the Qi Decoction, Great Qi-Coordinating Decoction, Daijoukito)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Emergency Dose**: Decoct above doses, take once and monitor response
**Preparation**: Cook Da Huang last (5 minutes), dissolve Mang Xiao in warm decoction
**Timing**: Usually given once, can repeat in 6-12 hours if no response
**Duration**: Single dose or very short-term use only

# **Xiao Cheng Qi Tang** (Minor Order the Qi Decoction, Small Coordinate Qi Decoction, Xiaochengqi Tang)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses, take warm once or twice daily as needed
**Preparation**: Da Huang added last 5-10 minutes of cooking to preserve active compounds
**Timing**: Usually taken in evening for morning bowel movement
**Duration**: Short-term use, 3-5 days maximum without medical supervision

# **Qing Zao Jiu Fei Tang** (Clear Dryness and Rescue the Lungs Decoction, Autumn Dryness Formula, Qingzao Jiufei Tang)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, add E Jiao at end of cooking (melt in)
**Timing**: Between meals with warm water, can increase fluid intake
**Duration**: 1-2 weeks for acute dryness, 4-8 weeks for chronic conditions

# **Xing Su San** (Apricot Kernel and Perilla Powder, Xingsu San)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, don't overcook to preserve volatile oils
**Timing**: After meals to protect stomach from Ban Xia
**Duration**: 5-10 days for acute conditions, longer for chronic cough

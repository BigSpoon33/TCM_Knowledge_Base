# **Su Zi Jiang Qi Tang** (Perilla Seed Decoction to Direct Qi Downward, Perilla Fruit Qi-Directing Decoction, Suzi Jiangqi Tang)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, Su Zi should be lightly crushed before cooking
**Timing**: Between meals, can increase frequency during cough episodes
**Duration**: 4-12 weeks for chronic conditions, often long-term maintenance

# **Gui Lu Er Xian Jiao** (Two-Immortal Decoction with Tortoise Shell and Deer Antler, Turtle and Deer Two Immortals Glue, Keirokunisenkyou)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Er Xian" (Two Immortals) refers to the legendary immortals who achieved longevity through balancing yin and yang. The addition of Gui Ban Jiao and Lu Jiao Jiao creates a powerful formula for kidney essence deficiency. Classic approach to aging and reproductive decline through dual tonification.

# **Qi Bao Mei Ran Dan** (Seven-Treasure Pill for Beautiful Whiskers, Hair Growth Formula, Qibao Meiran Dan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Qi Bao" means "seven treasures" referring to the seven precious herbs that work together for beauty and longevity. "Mei Ran" means "beautiful whiskers/hair" - emphasizing the formula's reputation for promoting healthy hair growth. Classic approach to premature aging through kidney essence and blood nourishment.

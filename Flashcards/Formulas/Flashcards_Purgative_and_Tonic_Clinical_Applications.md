# **Zeng Ye Cheng Qi Tang** (Increase the Fluids and Order the Qi Decoction, Fluid-Increasing Order Qi Decoction, Zengye Chengqi Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Yangming bowel heat with yin fluid deficiency
**Key Symptoms**: Constipation with dry hard stools, fever, thirst, dry mouth and throat, dark scanty urine, restlessness, possible delirium
**Tongue**: Red and dry with yellow coating
**Pulse**: Thin and rapid, or deep and rapid

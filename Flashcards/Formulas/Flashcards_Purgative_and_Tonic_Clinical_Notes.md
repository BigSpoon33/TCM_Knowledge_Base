# **Zeng Ye Cheng Qi Tang** (Increase the Fluids and Order the Qi Decoction, Fluid-Increasing Order Qi Decoction, Zengye Chengqi Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Zeng Ye Cheng Qi" means "increase fluids and order qi" - representing the balanced approach of nourishing yin fluids while gently purging accumulated heat. This formula addresses the common pattern where heat has damaged fluids, creating a vicious cycle of dryness and constipation.

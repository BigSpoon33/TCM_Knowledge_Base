# **Du Huo Ji Sheng Tang** (Pubescent Angelica and Loranthus Decoction, Independent Living Decoction, Dokukatsuiseito)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Classic formula for chronic wind-dampness affecting people with underlying constitutional weakness. "Du Huo" means "independent living" - referring to the ability to live independently despite chronic pain. The formula addresses both the pathogen (wind-dampness) and the constitutional deficiency that allows it to persist.

# **Juan Bi Tang** (Remove Painful Obstruction Decoction, Resolve Bi Syndrome Tang, Anti-Arthritic Decoction)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Juan Bi" means "remove painful obstruction" - referring to the formula's ability to clear blockages in channels and collaterals that cause joint pain. Classic approach to wind-dampness bi syndrome where external pathogenic factors obstruct qi and blood flow. The blood-nourishing herbs prevent further stagnation.

# **Shu Jing Huo Xue Tang** (Relax the Channels and Invigorate Blood Decoction, Channel-Dredging Blood-Moving Decoction, Shujing Huoxue Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Shu Jing Huo Xue" means "dredge channels and invigorate blood" - addressing blockages in the channel and collateral system. Comprehensive formula that combines blood nourishment with blood movement and qi regulation. Essential for chronic pain conditions where blood stasis combines with qi stagnation to block normal circulation.

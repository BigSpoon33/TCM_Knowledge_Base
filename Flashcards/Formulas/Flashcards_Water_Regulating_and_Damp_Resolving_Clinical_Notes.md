# **Wu Ling San** (Five-Ingredient Powder with Poria, Five Poria Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Master formula for water metabolism disorders. The combination of draining (Fu Ling, Zhu Ling, Ze Xie) with strengthening (Bai Zhu) and warming (Gui Zhi) creates a balanced approach that resolves dampness without damaging zheng qi.

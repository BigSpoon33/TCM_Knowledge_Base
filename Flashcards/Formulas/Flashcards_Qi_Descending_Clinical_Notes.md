# **Su Zi Jiang Qi Tang** (Perilla Seed Decoction to Direct Qi Downward, Perilla Fruit Qi-Directing Decoction, Suzi Jiangqi Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Su Zi Jiang Qi" means "perilla seed to direct qi downward" - addressing rebellious lung qi that fails to descend normally. Classic formula for chronic respiratory conditions with phlegm accumulation and underlying weakness. Balances qi direction with phlegm resolution and constitutional support.

# **Suan Zao Ren Tang** (Jujube Seed Decoction, Zizyphus Combination, Suanzaoren Tang)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Suan Zao Ren should be lightly crushed before decocting
**Timing**: Evening dose 1-2 hours before bedtime, morning dose if needed
**Duration**: 4-12 weeks for chronic insomnia, adjust as sleep improves

# **Tian Wang Bu Xin Dan** (Emperor of Heaven's Special Pill to Tonify the Heart, Heavenly Emperor Heart-Supplementing Elixir, Tianwang Buxin Dan)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Usually taken as pills/powder, 6-9g twice daily
**Preparation**: Traditionally made into honey pills, modern granules available
**Timing**: Evening dose 1-2 hours before bedtime, morning dose if needed
**Duration**: 8-16 weeks for chronic conditions, gradual reduction as symptoms improve

# **Chai Xian Tang** (Bupleurum and Pueraria Decoction, Chai Hu Ge Gen Tang, Exterior-Releasing Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Exterior wind-cold with shaoyang involvement and muscle tension
**Key Symptoms**: Neck and shoulder stiffness, headache, chills and fever, body aches, nasal congestion, mild sweating, tension in upper back
**Tongue**: Normal or slightly red with thin white coating
**Pulse**: Floating and wiry, or floating and tight

# **Xiao Chai Hu Tang** (Minor Bupleurum Decoction, Small Chai Hu Decoction, Xiao Chaihu Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Shaoyang stage disease with alternating chills and fever
**Key Symptoms**: Alternating chills and fever, bitter taste in mouth, dry throat, dizzy vision, chest and rib-side fullness, irritability, nausea, poor appetite
**Tongue**: White or thin yellow coating
**Pulse**: Wiry (xian mai)

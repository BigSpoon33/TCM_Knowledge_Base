# **Ban Xia Xie Xin Tang** (Pinellia Decoction to Drain the Epigastrium, Half-Summer Heart-Draining Decoction, Banxia Xiexin Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Xie Xin" means "drain the epigastrium" - referring to clearing the obstruction and disharmony in the middle jiao. This formula represents the classic approach to treating cold-heat complex patterns where hot and cold symptoms appear simultaneously. The genius lies in using both warming and cooling herbs together.

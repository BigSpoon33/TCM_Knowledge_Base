# **Liu Wei Di Huang Wan** (Six Ingredient Pill with Rehmannia, Liu Wei Di Huang Wan)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: 6-9g twice daily (prepared pills) or decoct above raw herb doses
**Preparation**: Pills preferred for long-term use, decoction for acute conditions
**Timing**: Between meals, preferably morning and evening
**Duration**: 3-6 months minimum for chronic conditions

# **Dang Gui Bu Xue Tang** (Tangkuei Decoction to Tonify the Blood, Angelica Blood-Tonifying Decoction, Tokishigyakukato)
## What are the details for **Cautions & Contraindications**?
?
- Yin deficiency with internal heat
- External pathogen invasion (acute cold/flu)
- Diarrhea from spleen yang deficiency
- Acute inflammatory conditions

- Severe blood deficiency without stasis signs

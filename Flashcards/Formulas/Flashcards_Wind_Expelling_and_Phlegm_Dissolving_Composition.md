# **Ban Xia Bai Zhu Tian Ma Tang** (Pinellia, Atractylodes, and Gastrodia Decoction, Gastrodia and Pinellia Combination, Phlegm-Transforming Wind-Calming Formula)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Cook Ban Xia longer to reduce toxicity, add Sheng Jiang
**Timing**: Between meals, can take with ginger tea
**Duration**: 2-8 weeks for acute symptoms, longer for chronic conditions

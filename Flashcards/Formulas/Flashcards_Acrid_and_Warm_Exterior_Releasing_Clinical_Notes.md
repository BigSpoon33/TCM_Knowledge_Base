# **Gui Zhi Tang** (Cinnamon Twig Decoction, Cassia Twig Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Classic "harmonizing" approach to exterior pathology. Unlike Ma Huang Tang's "opening" strategy, Gui Zhi Tang works through harmony - balancing ying and wei levels. The formula represents the gentlest approach to exterior release.

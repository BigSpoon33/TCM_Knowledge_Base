# **Er Chen Tang** (Two-Cured Decoction, Citrus and Pinellia Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Phlegm-dampness due to spleen qi deficiency
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic cough with profuse white phlegm, chest tightness, nausea, poor appetite, fatigue, feeling of fullness, dizziness
**Tongue**: Pale with thick white greasy coating
**Pulse**: Slippery or slippery and weak

# **Wen Dan Tang** (Warm the Gallbladder Decoction, Bamboo and Hoelen Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Phlegm-heat disturbing heart spirit and gallbladder
**Key Symptoms**: Insomnia, vivid dreams, nightmares, anxiety, irritability, dizziness, nausea, bitter taste in mouth, chest tightness, palpitations
**Tongue**: Red with yellow greasy coating
**Pulse**: Slippery and rapid, or wiry and slippery

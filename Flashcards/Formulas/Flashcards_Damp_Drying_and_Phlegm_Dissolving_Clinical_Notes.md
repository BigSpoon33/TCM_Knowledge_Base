# **Er Chen Tang** (Two-Cured Decoction, Citrus and Pinellia Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Foundation formula for all phlegm-dampness patterns. The name "Er Chen" (Two Cured) refers to the aged Chen Pi and Ban Xia, which are more effective when stored for years. Represents the basic approach to transforming phlegm at its source - the spleen.

# **Wen Dan Tang** (Warm the Gallbladder Decoction, Bamboo and Hoelen Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Warm the Gallbladder" doesn't mean adding heat, but rather clearing phlegm-heat that disturbs gallbladder's decision-making function. Classic formula for phlegm-heat affecting mental-emotional stability. The gallbladder controls courage and decision-making in TCM.

# **Ba Zhen Tang** (Eight Treasures Decoction, Eight Precious Formula, Tang-kuei and Ginseng Eight Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Simultaneous qi and blood deficiency
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic fatigue, pale complexion, dizziness, palpitations, shortness of breath, poor appetite, loose stools, irregular menstruation, cold hands and feet
**Tongue**: Pale with thin white coating
**Pulse**: Weak and thready, or weak and slow

# **Ba Zheng San** (Eight-Herb Powder for Rectification, Eight Orthodox Powder, Dianthus Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Bladder damp-heat with urinary difficulty
**Key Symptoms**: Painful urination, frequent urination, urgency, burning sensation, dark yellow urine, blood in urine, lower abdominal pain, fever, thirst
**Tongue**: Red with yellow coating
**Pulse**: Rapid and slippery, or rapid and wiry

# **Bai He Gu Jin Tang** (Lily Bulb Decoction to Preserve the Metal, Lily Preserve the Lungs Decoction, Fritillaria and Lily Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Lung and kidney yin deficiency with deficiency heat
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic dry cough, cough with scanty sputum or blood-streaked sputum, dry throat, hoarse voice, afternoon fever, night sweats, fatigue, weight loss
**Tongue**: Red with little coating or dry coating
**Pulse**: Thin and rapid, or weak and rapid

# **Bai Hu Tang** (White Tiger Decoction, Gypsum and Anemarrhena Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Qi level heat (Yang Ming heat) with injury to fluids
**Key Symptoms**: High fever (worse in afternoon), profuse sweating, intense thirst for cold drinks, irritability, restlessness, headache, toothache
**Tongue**: Red with yellow coating
**Pulse**: Surging and rapid, or flooding and large

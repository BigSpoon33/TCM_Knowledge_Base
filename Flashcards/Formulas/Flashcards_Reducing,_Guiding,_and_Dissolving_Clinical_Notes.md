# **Bao He Wan** (Preserve and Harmonize Pills, Citrus and Crataegus Formula, Food Stagnation Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Preserve and Harmonize" refers to preserving stomach function while harmonizing digestion. Classic formula for food stagnation - when people eat too much or inappropriate foods. The combination digests accumulated food while preventing heat generation from stagnation.

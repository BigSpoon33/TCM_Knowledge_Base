# **Yin Qiao San** (Honeysuckle and Forsythia Powder, Lonicera and Forsythia Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Classic formula for wind-heat exterior patterns. The combination of Jin Yin Hua and Lian Qiao creates the foundation for clearing heat and resolving toxicity while gently releasing the exterior. Represents the "cooling" approach to exterior release.

# **Zhi Sou San** (Stop Cough Powder, Antitussive Powder, Zhisou San)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Zhi Sou" means "stop cough" - this is the classic formula for chronic cough with external wind and internal phlegm. The combination addresses both the surface wind component and the deeper phlegm-dampness, making it ideal for lingering coughs that persist after acute illness.

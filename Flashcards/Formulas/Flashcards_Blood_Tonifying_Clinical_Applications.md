# **Dang Gui Bu Xue Tang** (Tangkuei Decoction to Tonify the Blood, Angelica Blood-Tonifying Decoction, Tokishigyakukato)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Blood deficiency due to qi deficiency (qi unable to generate blood)
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic fatigue, pale complexion, dizziness, palpitations, shortness of breath, poor appetite, loose stools, scanty menstruation, post-partum weakness
**Tongue**: Pale with thin white coating
**Pulse**: Weak and thin, or weak and slow

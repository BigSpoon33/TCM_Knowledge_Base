# **Tian Ma Gou Teng Yin** (Gastrodia and Uncaria Decoction, Tian Ma and Gou Teng Beverage, Tianma Gouteng Yin)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 9-12g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Gou Teng added near end of cooking to preserve alkaloids
**Timing**: Morning and evening doses, monitor blood pressure
**Duration**: 6-12 weeks for chronic conditions, often long-term maintenance

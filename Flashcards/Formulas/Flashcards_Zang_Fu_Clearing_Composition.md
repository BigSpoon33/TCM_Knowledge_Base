# **Long Dan Xie Gan Tang** (Gentiana Drain the Liver Decoction, Liver-Draining Formula)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, don't overcook to preserve active compounds
**Timing**: Between meals, can take 3 times daily for acute conditions
**Duration**: 1-3 weeks for acute conditions, stop when heat clears

# **Yu Nu Jian** (Jade Lady Decoction, Beautiful Lady Formula, Gypsum and Rehmannia Combination)

?

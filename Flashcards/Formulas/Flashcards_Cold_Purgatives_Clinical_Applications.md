# **Da Cheng Qi Tang** (Major Order the Qi Decoction, Great Qi-Coordinating Decoction, Daijoukito)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Yang ming fu syndrome - heat accumulation in large intestine
**Key Symptoms**: High fever, profuse sweating, severe constipation, abdominal distention and pain, delirium, restlessness, thirst, strong body odor
**Tongue**: Red or deep red with thick yellow dry coating
**Pulse**: Deep, forceful, and rapid

# **Xiao Cheng Qi Tang** (Minor Order the Qi Decoction, Small Coordinate Qi Decoction, Xiaochengqi Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Yangming bowel heat with qi stagnation (milder than Da Cheng Qi Tang)
**Key Symptoms**: Constipation, abdominal distension, mild abdominal pain, dry mouth, slight thirst, restlessness
**Tongue**: Yellow coating (less thick than Da Cheng Qi Tang)
**Pulse**: Rapid and forceful (less than Da Cheng Qi Tang)

# **Huo Xiang Zheng Qi San** (Agastache Powder to Rectify the Qi, Patchouli Qi-Correcting Powder, Koshosan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: External wind-cold with internal dampness affecting spleen and stomach
**Key Symptoms**: Summer flu, gastroenteritis, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, headache, fever with chills, chest oppression, poor appetite, fatigue
**Tongue**: White greasy coating
**Pulse**: Floating and slippery, or slow and slippery

# **Ping Wei San** (Calm the Stomach Powder, Magnolia and Ginger Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Spleen deficiency with dampness accumulation
**Key Symptoms**: Abdominal distension, poor appetite, nausea, feeling of fullness after eating, loose stools, heavy sensation in body and limbs, mental fogginess
**Tongue**: Thick white greasy coating
**Pulse**: Slow and slippery

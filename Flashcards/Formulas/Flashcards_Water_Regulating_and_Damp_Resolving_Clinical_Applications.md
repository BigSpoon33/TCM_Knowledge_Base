# **Wu Ling San** (Five-Ingredient Powder with Poria, Five Poria Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Water dampness retention with spleen yang deficiency
**Key Symptoms**: Edema, oliguria (scanty urination), abdominal distension, nausea, diarrhea, dizziness, palpitations, thirst but no desire to drink
**Tongue**: Pale with white coating
**Pulse**: Floating or deep and slow

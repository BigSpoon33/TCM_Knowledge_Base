# **Huan Shao Dan** (Rejuvenating Pill, Return to Youth Pill, Restoration Elixir)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Huan Shao" means "return to youth" - reflecting the formula's reputation for reversing signs of aging through kidney yang tonification. Classic approach to age-related decline focusing on the kidney as the root of vitality. The combination addresses both yang qi and blood to support comprehensive rejuvenation.

# **Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan** (Golden Cabinet Kidney Qi Pill, Kidney Qi Pill from the Golden Cabinet, Ba Wei Di Huang Wan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Classic formula that balances kidney yin and yang - using Liu Wei Di Huang Wan as foundation and adding small amounts of Gui Zhi and Fu Zi to warm yang qi. Essential concept: yang grows from yin, so must nourish yin foundation while gently warming yang. The "Golden Cabinet" refers to the Zhang Zhong Jing classic text.

# **You Gui Wan** (Right-Restoring Pill, Restore the Right Kidney Pill, You Gui Pills)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"You Gui" means "restore the right" where "right" refers to kidney yang. This formula represents Zhang Jing-yue's approach to kidney yang deficiency - nourish yin within yang tonification. More gentle than Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan, suitable for long-term use.

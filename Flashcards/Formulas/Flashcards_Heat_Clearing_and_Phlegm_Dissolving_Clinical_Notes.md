# **Qing Qi Hua Tan Wan** (Clear Qi and Transform Phlegm Pill, Heat-Phlegm Clearing Formula, Qingqi Huatan Wan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Classic formula for "phlegm-fire harassing the heart" patterns where heat-phlegm clouds the spirit and causes mental-emotional symptoms. Essential concept: phlegm and heat combine to obstruct clear thinking and emotional stability. The formula clears heat, transforms phlegm, and calms the spirit simultaneously.

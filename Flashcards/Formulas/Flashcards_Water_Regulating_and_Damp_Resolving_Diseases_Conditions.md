# **Wu Ling San** (Five-Ingredient Powder with Poria, Five Poria Formula)
## What **Diseases and Western Conditions** is this formula used for?
?
**TCM Diseases:**
- Acute and chronic nephritis
- Chronic diarrhea and gastroenteritis
- Congestive heart failure with edema
- Hypertension with fluid retention

**Western Conditions:**
- Inner ear disorders (dizziness, tinnitus)
- Metabolic syndrome with fluid retention

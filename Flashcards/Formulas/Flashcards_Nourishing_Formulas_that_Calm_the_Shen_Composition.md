# **Gan Mai Da Zao Tang** (Licorice, Wheat, and Jujube Decoction, Gan Mai Da Zao Tang)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, can increase Xiao Mai up to 60g for severe cases
**Preparation**: Gentle decoction (15-20 minutes), can be taken as syrup
**Timing**: Preferably between meals, extra dose during emotional crisis
**Duration**: 2-8 weeks for acute episodes, longer for chronic emotional instability

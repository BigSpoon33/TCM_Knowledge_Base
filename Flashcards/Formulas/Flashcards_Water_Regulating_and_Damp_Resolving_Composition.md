# **Wu Ling San** (Five-Ingredient Powder with Poria, Five Poria Formula)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or take as powder 6-9g twice daily
**Preparation**: Traditionally taken as powder mixed with warm water
**Timing**: Between meals, avoid taking too late in evening
**Duration**: 1-2 weeks for acute edema, longer for chronic conditions

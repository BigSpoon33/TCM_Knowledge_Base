# **Huang Lian Jie Du Tang** (Coptis Detoxification Decoction, Four Yellows Decoction)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Four Yellows" formula representing the ultimate heat-clearing and detoxifying combination. Each herb targets specific organs: Huang Lian (heart), Huang Qin (lung), Huang Bai (kidney), Zhi Zi (liver). Together they clear heat from all three jiao.

# **Qing Shang Fang Feng Tang** (Clear the Upper and Dispel Wind Decoction, Upper Jiao Wind-Heat Formula, Qingshang Fangfeng Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Qing Shang" means "clear the upper" referring to clearing heat from upper jiao, especially head and face regions. "Fang Feng" means "dispel wind" - addressing wind-heat patterns affecting sense organs. Classic approach for inflammatory conditions of head, face, and upper body with both heat and wind components.

# **Gui Zhi Tang** (Cinnamon Twig Decoction, Cassia Twig Tang)
## What are the details for **Cautions & Contraindications**?
?
- High fever without sweating (use Ma Huang Tang instead)
- Wind-heat patterns (fever higher than chills)
- Yin deficiency with night sweats
- Hypertension with liver yang rising
- Inflammatory skin conditions with heat signs

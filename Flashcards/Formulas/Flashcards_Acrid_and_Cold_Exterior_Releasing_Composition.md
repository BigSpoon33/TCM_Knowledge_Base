# **Yin Qiao San** (Honeysuckle and Forsythia Powder, Lonicera and Forsythia Formula)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, can take 3 times daily during acute phase
**Preparation**: Light decoction (10-15 minutes), don't overcook volatile oils
**Timing**: Every 4-6 hours during acute symptoms
**Duration**: 3-7 days, stop when exterior symptoms resolve

# **Zhi Sou San** (Stop Cough Powder, Antitussive Powder, Zhisou San)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, don't overcook to preserve volatile oils
**Timing**: Between meals, can take with honey for throat coating
**Duration**: 2-6 weeks for chronic cough, shorter for acute cough

# **Da Cheng Qi Tang** (Major Order the Qi Decoction, Great Qi-Coordinating Decoction, Daijoukito)
## What **Diseases and Western Conditions** is this formula used for?
?
**TCM Diseases:**
- Acute cholangitis and cholecystitis
- Functional intestinal obstruction
- Post-operative ileus
- Severe cases of diverticulitis
- Severe constipation and fecal impaction
- Toxic megacolon (as emergency support)

# **Xiao Cheng Qi Tang** (Minor Order the Qi Decoction, Small Coordinate Qi Decoction, Xiaochengqi Tang)
## What **Diseases and Western Conditions** is this formula used for?
?
**TCM Diseases:**
- Bowel preparation for procedures
- Chronic constipation in elderly
- Functional constipation
- Mild intestinal obstruction
- Post-operative ileus

**Western Conditions:**
- Irritable bowel syndrome with constipation

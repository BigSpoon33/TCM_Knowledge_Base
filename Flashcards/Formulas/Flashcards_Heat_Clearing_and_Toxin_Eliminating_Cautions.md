# **Huang Lian Jie Du Tang** (Coptis Detoxification Decoction, Four Yellows Decoction)
## What are the details for **Cautions & Contraindications**?
?
- Yang deficiency constitution
- Spleen yang deficiency with loose stools
- Pregnancy (strong heat-clearing herbs)
- Weak constitution without heat signs
- Chronic conditions without acute heat

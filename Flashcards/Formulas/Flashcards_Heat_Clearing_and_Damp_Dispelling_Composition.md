# **Gan Lu Xiao Du Dan** (Sweet Dew Special Pill to Eliminate Toxin, Dew-Clearing Detoxification Pill, Ganroshodokudan)

?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses 2-3 times daily, or 6-9g granules 2-3 times daily
**Preparation**: Cook minerals (Hua Shi) first, add Bo He last few minutes
**Timing**: Frequent small doses during acute phase
**Duration**: 1-2 weeks for acute conditions, monitor liver function

# **Yin Chen Wu Ling San** (Artemisia and Five-Ingredient Powder with Poria, Capillaris and Wuling San, Yinchen Wuling San)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, Yin Chen Hao can be increased for severe jaundice
**Timing**: Between meals for better absorption
**Duration**: 2-6 weeks for acute conditions, longer for chronic liver disease

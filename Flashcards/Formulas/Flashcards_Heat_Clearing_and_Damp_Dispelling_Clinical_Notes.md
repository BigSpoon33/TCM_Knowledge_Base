# **Gan Lu Xiao Du Dan** (Sweet Dew Special Pill to Eliminate Toxin, Dew-Clearing Detoxification Pill, Ganroshodokudan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Gan Lu" (Sweet Dew) refers to the beneficial moisture that resolves pathological dampness. Classic formula for epidemic diseases where external pathogen combines with internal dampness to create complex damp-heat patterns. The formula clears heat while transforming the turbid dampness that blocks normal qi movement.

# **Yin Chen Wu Ling San** (Artemisia and Five-Ingredient Powder with Poria, Capillaris and Wuling San, Yinchen Wuling San)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Yin Chen Wu Ling San" combines the liver-protective and jaundice-clearing action of Yin Chen Hao with the water-regulating power of Wu Ling San. This addresses the common pattern where liver damp-heat causes both jaundice and fluid retention. The formula treats both the local liver problem and the systemic water metabolism dysfunction.

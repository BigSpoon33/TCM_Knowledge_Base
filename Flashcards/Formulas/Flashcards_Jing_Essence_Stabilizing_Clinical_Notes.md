# **Jin Suo Gu Jing Wan** (Golden Lock Pill to Stabilize the Essence, Metal Lock Essence-Securing Pill, Jinsuo Gujing Wan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Jin Suo" means "golden lock" and "Gu Jing" means "stabilize essence" - referring to the formula's ability to lock and secure kidney essence. Classic approach to kidney deficiency where the kidney fails to secure essence, leading to various leakages. The combination of tonifying and astringent herbs addresses both root and manifestation.

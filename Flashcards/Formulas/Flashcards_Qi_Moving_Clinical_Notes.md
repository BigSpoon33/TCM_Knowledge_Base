# **Ban Xia Hou Po Tang** (Pinellia and Magnolia Bark Decoction, Banxia Houpo Decoction, Plum Stone Qi Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Plum Stone Qi" (Mei He Qi) describes the sensation of having a plum stone stuck in the throat - classic psychosomatic symptom. Formula treats the interaction between emotional stress and physical symptoms through regulating qi and transforming phlegm.

# **Yue Ju Wan** (Escape Restraint Pill, Six Stagnations Formula, Resolve Depression Pills)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Yue Ju" means "escape restraint" - referring to liberation from the six types of stagnation that commonly occur together. Each herb targets a specific type of stagnation: qi, dampness, blood, fire, and food. Classic formula representing comprehensive approach to stagnation patterns.

# **Mai Men Dong Tang** (Ophiopogon Decoction, Liriope Root Decoction, Maimendong Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Stomach yin deficiency with rebellious qi and heat
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic dry cough, nausea and vomiting, loss of appetite, dry mouth and throat, hiccups, retching, gastritis symptoms, thirst with desire for small sips
**Tongue**: Red and dry with little coating, or geographic tongue
**Pulse**: Thready and rapid, or weak and rapid

# **Qing Fei Tang** (Clear the Lungs Decoction, Lung-Clearing Formula, Qingfei Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Lung heat with phlegm and dryness
**Key Symptoms**: Productive cough with yellow phlegm, dry cough, chest tightness, fever, thirst, dry mouth and throat, shortness of breath, wheezing
**Tongue**: Red with yellow coating
**Pulse**: Rapid and slippery

# **Sha Shen Mai Men Dong Tang** (Adenophora and Ophiopogon Decoction, Glehnia and Liriope Combination, Shashen Maimendong Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Lung and stomach yin deficiency with residual dryness
**Key Symptoms**: Dry cough with little phlegm, dry mouth and throat, thirst, loss of appetite, dry retching, constipation, afternoon fever, night sweats, fatigue
**Tongue**: Red and dry with little coating
**Pulse**: Thready and rapid

# **Yu Quan Wan** (Jade Spring Pills, Jade Fountain Pills, Yuquan Wan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Lung and stomach heat with yin deficiency causing diabetes (Xiao Ke)
**Key Symptoms**: Excessive thirst, frequent urination, excessive hunger, weight loss, fatigue, dry mouth and throat, irritability, blurred vision
**Tongue**: Red with little coating or dry coating
**Pulse**: Rapid and weak, or thin and rapid

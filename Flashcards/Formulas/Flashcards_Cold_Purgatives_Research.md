# **Da Cheng Qi Tang** (Major Order the Qi Decoction, Great Qi-Coordinating Decoction, Daijoukito)
## What are the details for **Research & Evidence**?
?
- Clinical studies show efficacy in severe constipation and intestinal obstruction
- Research demonstrates strong laxative and anti-inflammatory effects
- Studies support use in acute cholecystitis and post-operative ileus

# **Huo Xiang Zheng Qi San** (Agastache Powder to Rectify the Qi, Patchouli Qi-Correcting Powder, Koshosan)
## What are the details for **Cautions & Contraindications**?
?

- Pure heat patterns without dampness
- Yin deficiency with internal heat
- Severe dehydration

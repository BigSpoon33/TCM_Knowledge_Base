# **Du Huo Ji Sheng Tang** (Pubescent Angelica and Loranthus Decoction, Independent Living Decoction, Dokukatsuiseito)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Chronic wind-dampness with liver-kidney deficiency and qi-blood weakness
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic joint pain (especially lower back and knees), stiffness worse in cold/damp weather, weakness in legs, chronic fatigue, pale complexion, cold limbs
**Tongue**: Pale with white coating
**Pulse**: Weak and slow, or weak and wiry

# **Juan Bi Tang** (Remove Painful Obstruction Decoction, Resolve Bi Syndrome Tang, Anti-Arthritic Decoction)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Wind-dampness obstructing channels and collaterals with blood stasis
**Key Symptoms**: Joint pain (worse in damp weather), stiffness, limited mobility, muscle aches, chronic arthritis, rheumatic pain that moves around, cold and heavy sensation in joints
**Tongue**: Normal or slightly red with white greasy coating
**Pulse**: Slow and slippery, or wiry and slippery

# **Shu Jing Huo Xue Tang** (Relax the Channels and Invigorate Blood Decoction, Channel-Dredging Blood-Moving Decoction, Shujing Huoxue Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Blood stasis with qi stagnation obstructing channels and collaterals
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic pain in joints and muscles, stiffness, numbness, limited range of motion, worse at night, dark complexion, possibly varicose veins
**Tongue**: Purple or with purple spots, may have thick coating
**Pulse**: Wiry and choppy, or deep and choppy

# **Gan Lu Xiao Du Dan** (Sweet Dew Special Pill to Eliminate Toxin, Dew-Clearing Detoxification Pill, Ganroshodokudan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Epidemic damp-heat with turbid dampness blocking the middle jiao
**Key Symptoms**: Fever with heavy head sensation, chest oppression, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, scanty dark urine, jaundice, sore throat, thick tongue coating
**Tongue**: Red with thick greasy yellow coating
**Pulse**: Slippery and rapid

# **Yin Chen Wu Ling San** (Artemisia and Five-Ingredient Powder with Poria, Capillaris and Wuling San, Yinchen Wuling San)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Liver and gallbladder damp-heat with water retention
**Key Symptoms**: Jaundice (yellow skin and eyes), abdominal distension, poor appetite, nausea, scanty urination, edema, fatigue, alternating chills and fever
**Tongue**: Yellow greasy coating
**Pulse**: Slippery and rapid, or soft and rapid

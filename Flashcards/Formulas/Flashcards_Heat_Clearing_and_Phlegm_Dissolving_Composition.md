# **Qing Qi Hua Tan Wan** (Clear Qi and Transform Phlegm Pill, Heat-Phlegm Clearing Formula, Qingqi Huatan Wan)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Traditionally taken as pills, or decoct above doses twice daily
**Preparation**: Pills preferred for convenience, decoction for acute symptoms
**Timing**: Between meals, evening dose may help with sleep
**Duration**: 2-8 weeks for mental symptoms, adjust based on response

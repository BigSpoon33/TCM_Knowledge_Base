# **Bao He Wan** (Preserve and Harmonize Pills, Citrus and Crataegus Formula, Food Stagnation Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Food stagnation in stomach with phlegm-dampness
**Key Symptoms**: Epigastric distention and fullness, belching with sour odor, nausea, loss of appetite, abdominal pain, diarrhea or constipation, thick tongue coating
**Tongue**: Thick white or yellow greasy coating
**Pulse**: Slippery or slippery and rapid

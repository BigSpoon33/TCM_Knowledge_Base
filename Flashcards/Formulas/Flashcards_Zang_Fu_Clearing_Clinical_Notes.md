# **Long Dan Xie Gan Tang** (Gentiana Drain the Liver Decoction, Liver-Draining Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Master formula for liver and gallbladder "damp-heat fire" patterns. Long Dan Cao is one of the strongest liver-clearing herbs. The formula balances clearing heat with nourishing blood (Dang Gui, Sheng Di) to prevent over-drying.

# **Qing Wei San** (Clear the Stomach Powder, Stomach Fire Clearing Formula, Qingwei San)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Clear the Stomach" refers to clearing fire from the stomach channel which manifests in oral and dental problems. Classic formula that balances fire-clearing with yin-blood nourishment to prevent over-drying. Essential for stomach fire patterns that affect the mouth, teeth, and gums.

# **Yu Nu Jian** (Jade Lady Decoction, Beautiful Lady Formula, Gypsum and Rehmannia Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Jade Lady" represents the cooling, nourishing quality of the formula - like a beautiful lady who is cool and composed. Treats the pattern of "upper heat, lower cold" where stomach fire flares upward due to kidney yin deficiency. Classic formula balancing clearing heat with nourishing yin.

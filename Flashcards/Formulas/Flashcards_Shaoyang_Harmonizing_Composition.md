# **Chai Xian Tang** (Bupleurum and Pueraria Decoction, Chai Hu Ge Gen Tang, Exterior-Releasing Combination)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, can increase Ge Gen for severe stiffness
**Timing**: During acute symptoms, can take 3 times daily initially
**Duration**: 3-7 days for acute conditions, 2-4 weeks for chronic tension

?

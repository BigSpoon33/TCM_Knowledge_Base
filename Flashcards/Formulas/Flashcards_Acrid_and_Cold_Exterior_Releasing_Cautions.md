# **Zhi Sou San** (Stop Cough Powder, Antitussive Powder, Zhisou San)
## What are the details for **Cautions & Contraindications**?
?
- Cough from lung yin deficiency without external wind
- Acute febrile illnesses requiring stronger exterior release
- Severe asthma attacks requiring emergency care
- Cough with significant blood in sputum

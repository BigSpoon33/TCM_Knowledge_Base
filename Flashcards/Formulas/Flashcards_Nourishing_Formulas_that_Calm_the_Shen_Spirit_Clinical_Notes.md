# **Suan Zao Ren Tang** (Jujube Seed Decoction, Zizyphus Combination, Suanzaoren Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Suan Zao Ren Tang" is the classic formula for insomnia from heart yin deficiency with internal heat. Suan Zao Ren (jujube seed) is the premier herb for nourishing heart yin and calming the spirit. The combination addresses both the deficiency (heart yin) and the manifestation (internal heat disturbing sleep).

# **Tian Wang Bu Xin Dan** (Emperor of Heaven's Special Pill to Tonify the Heart, Heavenly Emperor Heart-Supplementing Elixir, Tianwang Buxin Dan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Tian Wang Bu Xin Dan" means "Heavenly Emperor's heart-supplementing elixir" - representing the most comprehensive formula for heart-kidney yin deficiency with spirit disturbance. The 15-herb combination addresses all aspects of yin deficiency affecting the heart and spirit. Classic for severe insomnia and anxiety from constitutional weakness.

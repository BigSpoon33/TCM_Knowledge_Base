# **Da Cheng Qi Tang** (Major Order the Qi Decoction, Great Qi-Coordinating Decoction, Daijoukito)
## What are the details for **Therapeutic Actions**?
?

**Secondary**: Clear heat toxins, reduce fever, relieve abdominal distention, restore bowel function

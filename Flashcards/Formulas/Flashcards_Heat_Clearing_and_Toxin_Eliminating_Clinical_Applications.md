# **Huang Lian Jie Du Tang** (Coptis Detoxification Decoction, Four Yellows Decoction)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Triple jiao heat with toxicity and damp-heat
**Key Symptoms**: High fever, irritability, insomnia, red face, red eyes, sore throat, dark urine, constipation, skin eruptions, delirium
**Tongue**: Red with thick yellow coating
**Pulse**: Rapid and forceful

# **Qing Shang Fang Feng Tang** (Clear the Upper and Dispel Wind Decoction, Upper Jiao Wind-Heat Formula, Qingshang Fangfeng Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Wind-heat in upper jiao affecting head, face, and sense organs
**Key Symptoms**: Facial acne and skin eruptions, red swollen eyes, headaches, sinus congestion, ear infections, sore throat, dental pain, bad breath, facial redness and heat
**Tongue**: Red with yellow coating
**Pulse**: Rapid and floating, or rapid and wiry

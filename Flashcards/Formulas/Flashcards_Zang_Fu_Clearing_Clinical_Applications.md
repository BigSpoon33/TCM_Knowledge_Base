# **Long Dan Xie Gan Tang** (Gentiana Drain the Liver Decoction, Liver-Draining Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?

# **Qing Wei San** (Clear the Stomach Powder, Stomach Fire Clearing Formula, Qingwei San)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Stomach fire with yin and blood deficiency
**Key Symptoms**: Toothache, swollen bleeding gums, bad breath, mouth ulcers, red swollen tongue, thirst with desire for cold drinks, facial acne along jawline, irritability, constipation

# **Yu Nu Jian** (Jade Lady Decoction, Beautiful Lady Formula, Gypsum and Rehmannia Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Stomach fire flaring upward with kidney yin deficiency
**Key Symptoms**: Toothache, bleeding gums, loose teeth, frontal headache, irritability, thirst, dry mouth, red face, acid regurgitation
**Tongue**: Red with little coating or yellow coating
**Pulse**: Slippery and rapid, or weak and rapid

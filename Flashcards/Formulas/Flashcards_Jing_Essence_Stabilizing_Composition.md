# **Jin Suo Gu Jing Wan** (Golden Lock Pill to Stabilize the Essence, Metal Lock Essence-Securing Pill, Jinsuo Gujing Wan)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Traditionally taken as honey pills, or decoct above doses twice daily
**Preparation**: Powder form traditionally, can be pills, decoction, or granules
**Timing**: Morning and evening doses, or before situations requiring concentration
**Duration**: 1-3 months for acute issues, longer for constitutional support

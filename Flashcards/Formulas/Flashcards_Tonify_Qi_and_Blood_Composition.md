# **Jia Wei Gui Pi Tang** (Augmented Restore the Spleen Decoction, Modified Gui Pi Tang, Enhanced Spleen-Restoring Formula)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, can reduce Chai Hu if too dispersing
**Timing**: Morning and evening doses, evening dose 2 hours before bed
**Duration**: 1-3 months for chronic conditions, often used for maintenance

# **Su Zi Jiang Qi Tang** (Perilla Seed Decoction to Direct Qi Downward, Perilla Fruit Qi-Directing Decoction, Suzi Jiangqi Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Rebellious lung qi with phlegm accumulation, often with underlying deficiency
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic cough with wheeze, difficulty breathing, thick phlegm that's hard to expectorate, chest tightness, worse when lying down, fatigue
**Tongue**: May have thick coating
**Pulse**: Slippery or wiry and slippery

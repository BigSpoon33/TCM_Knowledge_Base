# **Ren Shen Bai Du San** (Ginseng Powder to Overcome Pathogenic Influences, Ginseng Anti-Toxin Powder, Renshen Baidu San)
## What **Diseases and Western Conditions** is this formula used for?
?
**TCM Diseases:**
- Chronic sinusitis and allergic rhinitis
- Immunocompromised patients with respiratory symptoms
- Recurrent upper respiratory infections
- Support during flu season for weak constitutions

**Western Conditions:**
- Chronic fatigue syndrome with frequent infections
- Post-viral fatigue syndrome

# **Ren Shen Yang Rong Tang** (Ginseng Decoction to Nourish the Construction, Ginseng Nutritive Combination, Renshen Yangrong Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Yang Rong" means "nourish the construction" referring to nourishing the constructive aspect of qi and blood that builds and maintains the body. Comprehensive formula that addresses qi, blood, heart, spleen, and kidney systems. Essential for people with severe constitutional weakness requiring multi-system support.

# **Shi Quan Da Bu Tang** (Perfect Great Tonifying Decoction, Ten Complete Great Supplementing Decoction, Shiquan Dabu Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Shi Quan Da Bu" means "perfect great tonification" - the most comprehensive qi and blood tonifying formula. Represents the ultimate constitutional support combining the strengths of Si Jun Zi Tang (qi) and Si Wu Tang (blood) with additional qi boosting (Huang Qi) and yang warming (Rou Gui). Essential for severe constitutional weakness.

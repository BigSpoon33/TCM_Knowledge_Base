# **Du Huo Ji Sheng Tang** (Pubescent Angelica and Loranthus Decoction, Independent Living Decoction, Dokukatsuiseito)
## What are the key **Symptoms** for this formula?
?
- Chronic joint pain (especially lower back and knees)
- Cold Limbs
- Fatigue
- Pain
- Pale Complexion
- Sciatica
- Weakness
- chronic fatigue
- cold limbs
- pale complexion
- stiffness worse in cold/damp weather
- weakness in legs

- Low Back Pain

- Sweating
- chronic arthritis
- cold and heavy sensation in joints
- limited mobility

- rheumatic pain that moves around

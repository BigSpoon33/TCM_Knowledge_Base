# **Huan Shao Dan** (Rejuvenating Pill, Return to Youth Pill, Restoration Elixir)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Kidney yang deficiency with reproductive decline and aging
**Key Symptoms**: Impotence, infertility, low libido, premature aging, fatigue, cold limbs, lower back pain, frequent urination, memory decline, hair loss
**Tongue**: Pale with white coating
**Pulse**: Deep, weak, and slow

# **Jin Gui Shen Qi Wan** (Golden Cabinet Kidney Qi Pill, Kidney Qi Pill from the Golden Cabinet, Ba Wei Di Huang Wan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Kidney yang deficiency with water metabolism disorders
**Key Symptoms**: Edema (especially lower body), frequent urination, nocturia, cold limbs, fatigue, lower back pain, impotence, infertility, diabetes with excessive urination, chronic nephritis
**Tongue**: Pale and swollen with white coating
**Pulse**: Deep, weak, and slow

# **You Gui Wan** (Right-Restoring Pill, Restore the Right Kidney Pill, You Gui Pills)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Kidney yang deficiency with essence depletion
**Key Symptoms**: Infertility, impotence, premature ejaculation, nocturnal emission, lower back pain, weak knees, cold limbs, frequent urination, chronic diarrhea, fatigue
**Tongue**: Pale with white coating
**Pulse**: Deep and weak, or deep and slow

# **Gui Lu Er Xian Jiao** (Two-Immortal Decoction with Tortoise Shell and Deer Antler, Turtle and Deer Two Immortals Glue, Keirokunisenkyou)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Dissolve gelatins in warm water, decoct other herbs, combine
**Preparation**: Dissolve animal gelatins separately, add to herbal decoction
**Timing**: Between meals, can divide into smaller frequent doses
**Duration**: 3-6 months minimum, often used long-term for maintenance

# **Qi Bao Mei Ran Dan** (Seven-Treasure Pill for Beautiful Whiskers, Hair Growth Formula, Qibao Meiran Dan)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Traditionally taken as honey pills, or decoct above doses twice daily
**Preparation**: Pills preferred for long-term use, decoction for acute cases
**Timing**: Morning and evening doses, can take with meals
**Duration**: 3-6 months minimum for hair growth effects, often long-term

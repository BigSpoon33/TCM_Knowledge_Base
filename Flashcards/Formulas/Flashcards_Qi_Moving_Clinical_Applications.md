# **Ban Xia Hou Po Tang** (Pinellia and Magnolia Bark Decoction, Banxia Houpo Decoction, Plum Stone Qi Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Phlegm-qi stagnation affecting throat and chest (Plum Stone Qi)
**Key Symptoms**: Sensation of something stuck in throat, chest tightness, difficulty swallowing, emotional instability, anxiety, depression, sighing, globus sensation
**Tongue**: Normal or slightly red with white coating
**Pulse**: Wiry or slippery

# **Yue Ju Wan** (Escape Restraint Pill, Six Stagnations Formula, Resolve Depression Pills)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Six stagnations (qi, blood, phlegm, fire, dampness, food)
**Key Symptoms**: Chest and epigastric distention, abdominal pain, bloating after eating, acid regurgitation, irritability, depression, anxiety, irregular bowel movements
**Tongue**: Normal or slightly red with white or yellow coating
**Pulse**: Wiry, or slippery and wiry

# **Gan Mai Da Zao Tang** (Licorice, Wheat, and Jujube Decoction, Gan Mai Da Zao Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Simplest yet most effective formula for emotional lability. The three ingredients work synergistically to create stability - Gan Cao harmonizes, Xiao Mai nourishes heart spirit, Da Zao strengthens the center. Represents the gentle approach to mental-emotional disorders.

# **Ren Shen Yang Rong Tang** (Ginseng Decoction to Nourish the Construction, Ginseng Nutritive Combination, Renshen Yangrong Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Severe qi and blood deficiency with heart-spleen weakness
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic fatigue, weakness, poor appetite, loose stools, insomnia, poor memory, anxiety, palpitations, shortness of breath, cold limbs, frequent illness
**Tongue**: Pale and thin with white coating
**Pulse**: Deep, weak, and slow

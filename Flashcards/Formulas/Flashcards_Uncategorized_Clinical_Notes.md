# **Ba Zhen Tang** (Eight Treasures Decoction, Eight Precious Formula, Tang-kuei and Ginseng Eight Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Eight Treasures" represents the perfect balance of qi and blood tonification. Simply combines the two foundation formulas (Si Jun Zi Tang + Si Wu Tang) to address simultaneous deficiency of both vital substances. Essential formula for severe constitutional weakness.

# **Ba Zheng San** (Eight-Herb Powder for Rectification, Eight Orthodox Powder, Dianthus Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Ba Zheng" means "eight rectifications" referring to the eight herbs that correct urinary dysfunction. Classic formula for acute bladder damp-heat patterns. The combination addresses both the heat component and the dampness accumulation in the lower jiao.

# **Bai He Gu Jin Tang** (Lily Bulb Decoction to Preserve the Metal, Lily Preserve the Lungs Decoction, Fritillaria and Lily Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Preserve the Metal" refers to nourishing the lung system (metal element). Classic formula for lung and kidney yin deficiency with chronic respiratory symptoms. The lily bulb (Bai He) is the signature herb for lung yin nourishment and respiratory protection.

# **Bai Hu Tang** (White Tiger Decoction, Gypsum and Anemarrhena Combination)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"White Tiger" represents the metal element - strong, cutting, cooling force that can subdue intense heat. This is the strongest heat-clearing formula for qi level patterns. The "four big" dosage pattern (large Shi Gao dose) is essential for effectiveness.

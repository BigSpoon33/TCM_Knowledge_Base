# **Qing Shang Fang Feng Tang** (Clear the Upper and Dispel Wind Decoction, Upper Jiao Wind-Heat Formula, Qingshang Fangfeng Tang)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, can be used for topical washes (cooled)
**Timing**: Between meals, can increase frequency for acute infections
**Duration**: 1-3 weeks for acute conditions, 6-12 weeks for chronic skin problems

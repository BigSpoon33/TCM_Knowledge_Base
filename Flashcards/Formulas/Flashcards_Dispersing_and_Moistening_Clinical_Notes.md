# **Qing Zao Jiu Fei Tang** (Clear Dryness and Rescue the Lungs Decoction, Autumn Dryness Formula, Qingzao Jiufei Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Qing Zao Jiu Fei" means "clear dryness and rescue the lungs" - addressing warm dryness that injures lung yin and fluids. Classic formula for autumn dryness syndrome where environmental dryness combines with heat to damage lung function. Balances cooling with moistening to restore normal lung physiology.

# **Xing Su San** (Apricot Kernel and Perilla Powder, Xingsu San)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Xing Su San" combines exterior releasing with phlegm transformation for the common pattern of catching cold while having underlying phlegm-dampness constitution. The apricot kernel and perilla combination specifically targets cough while addressing both the acute exterior condition and chronic internal dampness.

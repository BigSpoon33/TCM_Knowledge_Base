# **Ma Zi Ren Wan** (Cannabis Fruit Pill, Hemp Seed Pill, Ma Ren Wan)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Classic formula for "intestinal dryness" constipation where the intestines lack sufficient fluid to move stool normally. Ma Zi Ren provides natural oils to lubricate the bowels while other herbs regulate qi and gently promote movement. Represents the gentle, moistening approach to constipation.

# **Ban Xia Bai Zhu Tian Ma Tang** (Pinellia, Atractylodes, and Gastrodia Decoction, Gastrodia and Pinellia Combination, Phlegm-Transforming Wind-Calming Formula)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
Classic formula for phlegm-dampness generating internal wind, particularly affecting the head. The combination addresses the root (spleen deficiency creating phlegm) and the manifestation (wind symptoms like dizziness). Tian Ma is the key herb for calming internal wind.

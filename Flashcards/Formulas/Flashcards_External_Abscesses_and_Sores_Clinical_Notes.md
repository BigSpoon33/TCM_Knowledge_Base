# **Jing Fang Bai Du San** (Schizonepeta and Saposhnikovia Powder to Overcome Pathogenic Influences, Jingfang Baidu San, Anti-Toxin Powder)
## What are the details for **Clinical Notes & Commentary**?
?
### Traditional Understanding
"Bai Du" means "overcome poison" - referring to the formula's ability to eliminate various pathogenic factors that cause chronic skin problems and recurrent infections. Classic approach for people with weak defensive qi who are susceptible to external pathogenic factors. The formula strengthens the body's natural resistance.

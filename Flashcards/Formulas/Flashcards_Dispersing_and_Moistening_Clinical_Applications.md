# **Qing Zao Jiu Fei Tang** (Clear Dryness and Rescue the Lungs Decoction, Autumn Dryness Formula, Qingzao Jiufei Tang)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Warm dryness injuring lung yin with heat signs
**Key Symptoms**: Dry cough with little or no phlegm, dry mouth and throat, thirst, dry nose, headache, slight fever, irritability, dry skin, constipation
**Tongue**: Red and dry with little coating
**Pulse**: Rapid and thin, or floating and rapid

# **Xing Su San** (Apricot Kernel and Perilla Powder, Xingsu San)
## What are the details for **Clinical Applications**?
?
### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Exterior wind-cold with internal phlegm-dampness
**Key Symptoms**: Cough with white phlegm, chest tightness, mild chills and fever, headache, body aches, nasal congestion, poor appetite, fatigue
**Tongue**: White thick coating
**Pulse**: Floating and slippery

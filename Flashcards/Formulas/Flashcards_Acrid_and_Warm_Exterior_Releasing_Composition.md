# **Gui Zhi Tang** (Cinnamon Twig Decoction, Cassia Twig Tang)
## What are the details for **Dosage & Administration**?
?
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses, drink warm, cover with blanket to promote mild sweating
**Preparation**: Light decoction (10-15 minutes), strain and drink warm
**Timing**: Every 2-3 hours during acute phase, 2-3 times daily for chronic conditions
**Duration**: 1-3 days for acute cold, 2-4 weeks for chronic conditions

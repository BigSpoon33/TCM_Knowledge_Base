# **SI-1** · SHÀOZÉ (少澤) · *Lesser Marsh*
## What are the clinical indications for this point?
?
**Musculoskeletal:**
  - Stiff neck
  - pain of the postero-lateral shoulder and upper arm
  - pain of the elbow
  - trembling and numbness of the arm
  - disorders of the little finger

**Sensory:**
  - superficial visual obstruction
  - red eyes
  - nosebleed
  - deafness
  - tinnitus
  - heat in the mouth
  - erosion of the mouth
  - mouth ulcers
  - drooling

**Head Throat:**
  - Headache
  - dizziness
  - throat painful obstruction
  - curled tongue
  - stiff tongue

**Mental Emotional:**
  - Loss of consciousness from windstroke
  - mania disorder
  - clonic spasm

**Other:**
  - cold sensation below the Heart
  - agitation with Heart pain
  - oppression and pain of the chest
  - pain of the lateral costal region
  - Cold shivering
  - chills and fever with absence of sweating
  - febrile disease
  - malaria
  - cough
  - Swelling of the breast
  - breast abscess
  - absence of lactation

# **DU-1** · CHÁNGQIÁNG (長強) · *Long Strong*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the midline, midway between the tip of the coccyx and the anus.

**Notes:** This point may be located and needled with the patient either: i. lying on the front, ii. lying on the side with knees drawn up, or iii. first sitting on the heels and then leaning forwards and resting the upper body weight on the elbows with the head on the hands.

**Special Properties:**
- Luo-Connecting point of the Governing vessel
- Meeting point of the Governing vessel with the Conception vessel and the Gall Bladder and Kidney channels

# **DU-20** · BǍIHUÌ (百會) · *Hundred Meetings*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** At the vertex on the midline, in the depression 5 cun posterior to the anterior hairline and 7 cun superior to the posterior hairline. This point may also be measured as 8 cun posterior to the glabella and 6 cun superior to the external occipital protuberance.

**Notes:** i. Place the heels of the hands on the anterior and posterior hairlines and extend the middle fingers towards each other; Bǎihuì DU-20 is located 1 cun anterior to where the middle fingers meet; ii. If the anterior hairline is indistinct, place the heels of the hands on the glabella and external occipital protuberance and extend the middle fingers towards each other; Bǎihuì DU-20 is located 1 cun posterior to where the middle fingers meet; iii. Extend a line along the long axis of the ear (from the midpoint of the lobe to the midpoint of the apex) in its slightly posterior direction to the top of the head where Bǎihuì DU-20 may be found.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Governing vessel with the Bladder, Gall Bladder, Sanjiao and Liver channels
- Point of the Sea of Marrow
- Location of the upper dantian (cinnabar field)
- Echoes Heaven

# **DU-21** · QIÁNDǏNG (前頂) · *In Front of the Crown*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** At the top of the head on the midline, 1.5 cun directly anterior to Bǎihuì DU-20 and 3.5 cun posterior to the anterior hairline.

**Notes:** i. Divide the distance between Bǎihuì DU-20 and the anterior hairline into thirds. Qiándǐng DU-21 lies slightly posterior to the junction of the first and middle thirds; ii. If the anterior hairline is indistinct, locate it 3 cun superior to the glabella and 5 cun anterior to Bǎihuì DU-20.

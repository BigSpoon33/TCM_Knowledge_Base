# **BL-14** · JUÉYĪNSHŪ (厥陰俞) · *Jueyin Shu*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Oblique insertion towards the spine, or transverse-oblique insertion.
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun (oblique), 1 to 1.5 cun (transverse-oblique)

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- perpendicular needling or oblique needling away from the spine carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

# **P-1** · TIĀNCHÍ (天池) · *Heavenly Pool*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Transverse-oblique insertion posteriorly along the intercostal space
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- deep needling carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax

# **P-3** · QŪZÉ (曲澤) · *Marsh at the Crook*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Perpendicular insertion or prick to bleed
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- The brachial artery and veins lie deeply, just medial to this point.

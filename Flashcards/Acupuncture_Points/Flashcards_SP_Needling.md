# **SP-1** · YǏNBÀI (隱白) · *Hidden White*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Perpendicular or oblique insertion directed proximally, or prick to bleed.
**Depth:** 0.1 to 0.2 cun

# **SP-12** · CHŌNGMÉN (沖門) · *Rushing Gate*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Perpendicular insertion
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**

# **SP-15** · DÀHÉNG (大横) · *Great Horizontal*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Perpendicular insertion
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- in thin patients deep needling may penetrate the peritoneal cavity
- deep needling at this point may penetrate a substantially enlarged spleen or liver
<!--SR:!2025-11-10,3,250-->

# **SP-17** · SHÍDÒU (食竇) · *Food Cavity*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Transverse-oblique insertion along the intercostal space
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- perpendicular insertion, especially in thin patients, carries a substantial risk of inducing a pneumothorax

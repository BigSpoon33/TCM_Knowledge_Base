# **SP-1** · YǏNBÀI (隱白) · *Hidden White*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the dorsal aspect of the big toe, at the junction of lines drawn along the medial border of the nail and the base of the nail, approximately 0.1 cun from the corner of the nail.

**Special Properties:**
- Jing-Well point
- Wood point
- Sun Si-miao Ghost point

# **SP-10** · XUÈHǍI (血海) · *Sea of Blood*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** 2 cun proximal to the superior border of the patella, in the tender depression on the bulge of the vastus medialis muscle, directly above Yīnlíngquán SP-9.

**Notes:** i. Place the heel of your right palm on the inferior border of the patient's left patella, with the fingers and thumb fully extended and the thumb at 45° to the index finger. This point is found beneath the tip of the thumb, directly above Yīnlíngquán SP-9; ii. Locate one patella's height (2 cun) above the superior border of the patella, in the tender depression directly above Yīnlíngquán SP-9.

# **SP-11** · JĪMÉN (箕門) · *Winnowing Gate*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the medial side of the upper leg, 6 cun proximal to Xuèhǎi SP-10, on a line connecting Xuèhǎi SP-10 with Chōngmén SP-12.

**Notes:** i. Locate two handbreadths superior to Xuèhǎi SP-10; ii. Locate midway between the tibiofemoral joint line and Chōngmén SP-12.

# **SP-12** · CHŌNGMÉN (沖門) · *Rushing Gate*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** 3.5 cun lateral to Qūgǔ REN-2, on the lateral side of the femoral artery.

**Notes:** Palpate to locate the pulsation of the femoral artery, just over one handbreadth lateral to the midline, at the level of the upper border of the pubic symphysis. Locate Chōngmén SP-12 in the depression immediately lateral to this pulsation.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Spleen and Liver channels with the Yin Linking vessel
- Coalescent point of the Yin Linking vessel

# **SP-13** · FǓSHÈ (府舍) · *Abode of the Fu*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the lower abdomen, 0.7 cun superior and 0.5 cun lateral to Chōngmén SP-12, 4 cun lateral to the midline. This point is also defined as lying 4.3 cun inferior to Dàhéng SP-15.

**Notes:** The 4 cun line is determined by the palpable lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle at the level of the umbilicus.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Spleen and Liver channels with the Yin Linking vessel

# **SP-15** · DÀHÉNG (大横) · *Great Horizontal*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the abdomen, in the depression at the lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle level with the umbilicus.

**Notes:** This point is normally defined as 4 cun lateral to the midline, and on the abdomen the 4 cun line is located at the lateral border of the rectus abdominis muscle.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Spleen channel with the Yin Linking vessel

# **SP-17** · SHÍDÒU (食竇) · *Food Cavity*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the lateral side of the chest, in the fifth intercostal space, 6 cun lateral to the midline.

**Notes:** i. The intercostal spaces are most reliably counted downwards from the second intercostal space which lies immediately below the sternal angle. In males, the nipple almost invariably lies in the fourth intercostal space; ii. The six cun line is located two cun lateral to the mamillary line.

# **SP-20** · ZHŌURÓNG (周榮) · *Encircling Glory*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the lateral side of the chest, in the second intercostal space, 6 cun lateral to the midline.

**Notes:** i. First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, then locate the second intercostal space below it; ii. The six cun line is located two cun lateral to the mamillary line; iii. This point lies one intercostal space directly below Zhōngfǔ LU-1.

# **SP-21** · DÀBĀO (大包) · *Great Wrapping*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the mid-axillary line, in the seventh intercostal space. Note: some sources locate this point in the sixth intercostal space.

**Notes:** i. The intercostal spaces are most reliably counted downwards from the second intercostal space which lies immediately below the sternal angle. In males, the nipple almost invariably lies in the fourth intercostal space; ii. the mid-axillary line is drawn vertically down from the apex of the axilla (Jíquán HE-1).

**Special Properties:**
- Great Luo-Connecting point of the Spleen
- embraces the blood of all the luo-connecting channels

# **SP-6** · SĀNYĪNJIĀO (三陰交) · *Three Yin Intersection*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the medial side of the lower leg, 3 cun superior to the prominence of the medial malleolus, in a depression close to the medial crest of the tibia.

**Notes:** This point is most readily located one handbreadth superior to the prominence of the medial malleolus.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Spleen, Liver and Kidney channels

# **SP-7** · LÒUGǓ (漏谷) · *Dripping Valley*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the medial side of the lower leg, 3 cun superior to Sānyīnjiāo SP-6, in a depression just posterior to the medial crest of the tibia.

**Notes:** This point is most readily located one handbreadth superior to Sānyīnjiāo SP-6.

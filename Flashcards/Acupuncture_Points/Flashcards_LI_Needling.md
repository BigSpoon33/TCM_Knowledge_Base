# **L.I.-15** · JIĀNYÚ (肩髃) · *Shoulder Bone*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** i. With the arm abducted, perpendicular insertion directed towards the centre of the axilla; ii. Transverse-oblique insertion directed distally towards the elbow.
**Depth:** 1 to 1.5 cun (perpendicular insertion); 1.5 to 2 cun (transverse-oblique insertion)
<!--SR:!2025-11-10,3,250-->

# **L.I.-17** · TIĀNDǏNG (天鼎) · *Heaven's Tripod*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Perpendicular insertion; Oblique insertion
**Depth:** 0.3 to 0.5 cun (perpendicular); 0.5 to 0.8 cun (oblique)

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- deeper needling may puncture the carotid artery or jugular vein

# **L.I.-19** · KǑUHÉLIÁO (口禾髎) · *Mouth Grain Crevice*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Oblique insertion
**Depth:** 0.3 to 0.5 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- according to some classical and modern texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion

# **L.I.-20** · YÍNGXIĀNG (迎香) · *Welcome Fragrance*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Transverse insertion medio-superiorly or join to Bitong (M-HN-14) at the highest point of the naso-labial groove.
**Depth:** 0.3 to 0.5 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- Contraindicated to moxibustion
<!--SR:!2025-11-10,3,250-->

# **L.I.-4** · HÉGǓ (合谷) · *Joining Valley*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** i. Perpendicular insertion 0.5 to 1 cun; ii. Oblique insertion directed proximally 1 to 1.5 cun.
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun (perpendicular); 1 to 1.5 cun (oblique proximally)

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- Contraindicated in pregnancy

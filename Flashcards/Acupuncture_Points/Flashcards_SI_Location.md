# **SI-1** · SHÀOZÉ (少澤) · *Lesser Marsh*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the dorsal aspect of the little finger, at the junction of lines drawn along the ulnar border of the nail and the base of the nail, approximately 0.1 cun from the corner of the nail.

**Special Properties:**
- Jing-Well
- Metal point

# **SI-10** · NĀOSHŪ (臑俞) · *Upper Arm Shu*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the posterior aspect of the shoulder, in the depression inferior to the scapular spine, directly superior to the posterior axillary crease when the arm hangs in the adducted position.

**Notes:** Slide a finger directly upwards from Jiānzhēn SI-9 until it falls into the depression just below the scapular spine.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Small Intestine and Bladder channels with the Yang Linking and Yang Motility vessels

# **SI-11** · TIĀNZŌNG (天宗) · *Heavenly Gathering*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the scapula, in a tender depression one third of the distance from the midpoint of the inferior border of the scapular spine to the inferior angle of the scapula.

**Notes:** In practice it may be difficult to palpate the inferior angle of the scapula. An alternative method of location is to draw an equilateral triangle with Jiānzhēn SI-9 and Nāoshū SI-10, after first ensuring that the patient's shoulder is relaxed.

# **SI-12** · BǏNGFĒNG (秉風) · *Grasping the Wind*

?
**Location:** In the centre of the suprascapular fossa, directly above Tiānzōng SI-11, in a depression formed when the arm is raised.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Small Intestine, Large Intestine, Sanjiao and Gall Bladder channels

# **SI-13** · QŪYUÁN (曲垣) · *Crooked Wall*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** In the tender depression superior to the medial end of the scapular spine, midway between Nāoshū SI-10 and the spinous process of T2.

**Notes:** Slide a fingertip medially along the superior border of the scapular spine until it falls into the tender depression just lateral to the medial border of the scapula.

# **SI-16** · TIĀNCHUĀNG (天窗) · *Heavenly Window*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle, level with the laryngeal prominence.

**Notes:** i. Palpation of the posterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle is made easier if the patient turns their head away from the side to be needled, whilst you apply resistance at the chin; ii. In females the laryngeal prominence is not as pronounced as in males. If it is indistinct, palpate the depression formed by the lower border of the hyoid bone and the upper border of the thyroid cartilage at the midline. The laryngeal prominence lies just below this.

**Special Properties:**
- Window of Heaven point

# **SI-17** · TIĀNRÓNG (天容) · *Heavenly Appearance*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** In the depression between the angle of the mandible and the anterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle.

**Notes:** i. Palpation of the anterior border of the sternocleidomastoid muscle is made easier if the patient turns their head away from the side to be needled, whilst you apply resistance at the chin; ii. If the transverse process of C2 is palpable, locate and needle this point anterior to it.

**Special Properties:**
- Point of the Window of Heaven

# **SI-19** · TĪNGGŌNG (聽宮) · *Palace of Hearing*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** With the mouth open, this point is located in the depression between the middle of the tragus and the condyloid process of the mandible.

**Notes:** In order to locate this point, ask the patient to open the mouth so that the condyloid process of the mandible slides forwards to reveal the depression.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Small Intestine, Sanjiao and Gall Bladder channels

# **SI-3** · HÒUXĪ (後谿) · *Back Stream*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the ulnar border of the hand, in the substantial depression proximal to the head of the fifth metacarpal bone.

# **SI-5** · YÁNGGǓ (陽谷) · *Yang Valley*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** At the ulnar border of the wrist, in the depression between the head of the ulna and the triquetral bone.

**Notes:** Most sources locate this point between the styloid process of the ulna and the triquetral bone, but in practice, the styloid process of the ulna may not be palpable and where it is palpable it often lies slightly posterior to Yánggǔ SI-5.

**Special Properties:**
- Jing-River
- Fire point

# **SI-6** · YǍNGLǍO (養老) · *Support the Aged*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** When the palm of the hand is placed on the chest, this point is located on the dorsal aspect of the head of the ulna, in a cleft level with and to the radial side of the high point of the styloid process of the ulna.

**Notes:** This point may be located in the following way. Ask the patient to lie with their arm by their side and the hand prone (palm on the couch). If the practitioner places a finger on the high point of the ulnar styloid and asks the patient to place their palm on their chest, the point is found in the cleft where the practitioner's finger then rests.

**Special Properties:**
- Xi-Cleft point of the Small Intestine channel

# **SI-7** · ZHĪZHÈNG (支正) · *Branch of the Upright*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On a line connecting Yánggǔ SI-5 and Xiǎohǎi SI-8, 5 cun proximal to Yánggǔ SI-5, in the groove between the anterior border of the ulna and the muscle belly of flexor carpi ulnaris.

**Notes:** This point should be located and needled with the patient lying on their back and with their arm either in the supine position or resting on the chest.

**Special Properties:**
- Luo-Connecting point of the Small Intestine channel

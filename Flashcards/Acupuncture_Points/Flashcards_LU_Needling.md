# **LU-1** · ZHŌNGFǓ (中府) · *Middle Palace*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Transverse-oblique insertion medially along the intercostal space.
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- Deep perpendicular or oblique insertion carries a substantial risk of causing a pneumothorax.

# **LU-3** · TIĀNFǓ (天府) · *Palace of Heaven*
## What is the needling technique and cautions for this point?
?
**Method:** Perpendicular insertion
**Depth:** 0.5 to 1 cun

**⚠️ Cautions:**
- According to several classical texts, this point is contraindicated to moxibustion. The Systematic Classic of Acupuncture and Moxibustion says that if moxa is used it will cause counterflow and disordered qi.

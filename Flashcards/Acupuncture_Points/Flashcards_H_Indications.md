# **HE-1** · JÍQUÁN (極泉) · *Summit Spring*
## What are the clinical indications for this point?
?
**Respiratory:**
  - shortness of breath
  - distention and fullness of the lateral costal region

**Head Throat:**
  - dry throat
  - yellow eyes

**Pain:**
  - Heart painful obstruction
  - Heart pain with retching
  - chest pain
  - pain of the axilla
  - numbness and painful obstruction of the upper limb
  - inversion counterflow of the elbow and arm
  - cold and pain of the elbow and arm

**Other:**
  - sadness and anxiety
  - palpitations
  - agitation with thirst
  - Inability to raise the shoulder
  - scrofula
  - loss of use of the four limbs

# **HE-2** · QĪNGLÍNG (青靈) · *Green Spirit*
## What are the clinical indications for this point?
?
**Head Throat:**
  - headache with cold shivering

**Pain:**
  - Inability to raise the shoulder and arm
  - swelling, pain and redness of the shoulder
  - pain of the axilla
  - pain of the lateral costal region

**Other:**
  - yellow eyes
  - scrofula

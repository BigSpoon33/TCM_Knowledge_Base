# **BL-19** · DǍNSHŪ (膽俞) · *Gall bladder Shu*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** 1.5 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the tenth thoracic vertebra (T10).

**Notes:** Locate at the visible highest point of the paraspinal muscles.

- Back-Shu point of the Gall Bladder

# **GB-15** · TÓULÍNQÌ (頭臨泣) · *Head Governor of Tears*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the forehead, directly above Yángbái GB-14, 0.5 cun within the anterior hairline, midway between Shéntíng DU-24 and Tóuwéi ST-8.

**Notes:** The distance between the anterior and posterior hairlines in the midline is measured as 12 cun. If the anterior hairline is indistinct, the distance is measured as 15 cun between the glabella [point Yìntáng M-HN-3] and the posterior hairline; the location of the anterior hairline would thus be defined as one fifth of this distance. If the posterior hairline is indistinct, it can be measured as 1 cun inferior to Fēngfǔ DU-16 which lies immediately below the external occipital protuberance. Note that the line of the anterior hairline away from the midline usually curves somewhat posteriorly, and this should be taken into account when locating the Gall Bladder points on the scalp (Tóulínqì GB-15 to Nǎokōng GB-19).

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder and Bladder channels with the Yang Linking vessel

## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** In the hollow between the intertragic notch posteriorly and the condyloid process of the mandible anteriorly. Locate this point with the mouth wide open.

**Notes:** In order to locate this point, ask the patient to open the mouth so that the condyloid process of the mandible slides forwards to reveal the depression.

# **GB-22** · YUĀNYÈ (淵腋) · *Armpit Abyss*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the mid-axillary line, in the fifth intercostal space, approximately 3 cun inferior to the apex of the axilla, at the level of the nipple.

**Notes:** i. The mid-axillary line is drawn vertically down from the apex of the axilla (Jíquán HE-1); ii. First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, locate the second intercostal space below it and then locate the fifth intercostal space, three spaces below that; in males the nipple lies in the fourth intercostal space; iii. Some sources locate this point in the fourth intercostal space.

# **GB-25** · JĪNGMÉN (京門) · *Capital Gate*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** Below the lateral aspect of the ribcage, anterior and inferior to the free end of the 12th rib.

**Notes:** To locate the free end of the twelfth rib, first place the entire hand on the upper abdomen and with gentle finger pressure palpate downwards along the costal margin, until the end of the eleventh rib is located just above the level of the umbilicus. Then palpate further along the inferior margin of the ribcage until the free end of the twelfth rib is located in the lateral lumbar region.

**Special Properties:**
- Front-Mu point of the Kidneys

# **GB-29** · JŪLIÁO (居髎) · *Stationary Crevice*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the lateral aspect of the hip joint, at the midpoint of a line drawn between the anterior superior iliac spine and the prominence of the greater trochanter.

**Notes:** i. To locate the anterior superior iliac spine see Wǔshū GB-27; ii. To accurately palpate the prominence of the greater trochanter place one hand over the lateral aspect of the hip joint at the level of the perineum, and with the other hand rotate the foot in order to feel the movement of the prominence of the greater trochanter; iii. Alternatively, flex the hip joint and locate Jūliáo GB-29 at the lateral end of the crease thus formed.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder channel with the Yang Motility vessel

# **GB-30** · HUÁNTIÀO (環跳) · *Jumping Circle*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the postero-lateral aspect of the hip joint, one third of the distance between the prominence of the greater trochanter and the sacro-coccygeal hiatus (Yāoshū DU-2).

**Notes:** i. This point may most readily be located (and needled) with the patient lying on their side; ii. To accurately palpate the prominence of the greater trochanter place one hand over the lateral aspect of the hip joint at the level of the perineum, and with the other hand rotate the foot in order to feel the movement of the prominence of the greater trochanter; iii. The sacro-coccygeal hiatus is located in-between the cornua of the sacrum and coccyx, in the depression inferior to the fourth sacral spinous process if this is palpable. Note, however, that the sacro-coccygeal hiatus may sometimes extend as high as the level of the third sacral foramina.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Gall Bladder and Bladder channels
- Ma Dan-yang Heavenly Star point

# **GB-31** · FĒNGSHÌ (風市) · *Wind Market*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the lateral aspect of the thigh, directly below the greater trochanter, 7 cun superior to the popliteal crease.

**Notes:** i. To palpate the prominence of the greater trochanter place a hand over the lateral aspect of the hip joint at the level of the perineum; ii. The distance between the prominence of the greater trochanter and the popliteal crease is 19 cun, thus Fēngshì GB-31 is located at the tender point approximately 1 cun proximal to the junction of the upper two thirds and lower third of this line; iii. This point is traditionally described as being located where the tip of the middle finger reaches when a person stands erect with their arms extended by their sides, although the significant differences found in body proportions render this unreliable.

?

# **GB-35** · YÁNGJIĀO (陽交) · *Yang Intersection*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the lateral aspect of the lower leg, 7 cun superior to the prominence of the lateral malleolus, in the depression at the posterior border of the fibula.

**Notes:** i. The distance between the tip of the lateral malleolus and the popliteal crease is 16 cun; locate Yángjiāo GB-35 one cun distal to the midpoint of this line; ii. Above the region of Xuánzhōng GB-39, the fibula is not easily palpable because it is covered by the peroneus brevis muscle; therefore feel for the posterior border of the fibula above the malleolus where it is easily palpable and extend a line towards the head of the fibula.

**Special Properties:**
- Xi-Cleft point
- Point of Yang Linking vessel

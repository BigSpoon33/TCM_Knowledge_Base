# **BL-14** · JUÉYĪNSHŪ (厥陰俞) · *Jueyin Shu*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** 1.5 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the fourth thoracic vertebra (T4).

**Notes:** Locate at the visible highest point of the paraspinal muscles.

**Special Properties:**
- Back-Shu point of the Pericardium

# **P-1** · TIĀNCHÍ (天池) · *Heavenly Pool*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** 1 cun lateral and slightly superior to the nipple, in the 4th intercostal space.

**Notes:** The level of the fourth intercostal space at this point is more or less the same as that of the fourth intercostal space at its junction with the sternum.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Pericardium, Gall Bladder, Liver and Sanjiao channels
- Point of the Window of Heaven

# **P-3** · QŪZÉ (曲澤) · *Marsh at the Crook*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the transverse cubital crease, in the depression immediately to the ulnar side of the aponeurosis of the biceps brachii muscle.

**Notes:** This point should be located and needled with the elbow slightly bent.

**Special Properties:**
- He-Sea point
- Water point

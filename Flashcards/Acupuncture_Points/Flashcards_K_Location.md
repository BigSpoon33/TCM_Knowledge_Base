# **BL-23** · SHÈNSHŪ (腎俞) · *Kidney Shu*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** 1.5 cun lateral to the lower border of the spinous process of the second lumbar vertebra (L2).

**Notes:** Locate at the visible highest point of the paraspinal muscles.

**Special Properties:**
- Back-Shu point of the Kidneys

# **KID-11** · HÉNGGǓ (橫骨) · *Pubic Bone*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the lower abdomen, 5 cun below the umbilicus, at the superior border of the symphysis pubis, 0.5 cun lateral to the midline (Qūgǔ REN-2).

**Notes:** The Great Compendium of Acupuncture and Moxibustion locates the points Hénggǔ KID-11 to Shāngqū KID-17 one cun lateral to the midline.

**Special Properties:**
- Meeting point of the Kidney channel with the Penetrating vessel

# **KID-22** · BÙLÁNG (步廊) · *Walking Corridor*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** In the fifth intercostal space, 2 cun lateral to the midline.

**Notes:** i. First locate the second intercostal space (see Shéncáng KID-25), then find the fifth intercostal space, three spaces below it; ii. Note that in males the nipple lies in the fourth intercostal space; iii. The 2 cun line is located midway between the midline and the mamillary line.

**Special Properties:**
- One of the twelve shu points of the chest

## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** In the second intercostal space, 2 cun lateral to the midline.

**Notes:** i. First locate the costal cartilage of the second rib which is level with the sternal angle, then locate the second intercostal space below it, ii. # **KID-6** · ZHÀOHǍI (照海) · *Shining Sea*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** 1 cun below the prominence of the medial malleolus, in the groove formed by two ligamentous bundles.

**Notes:** This point lies between the tibialis posterior tendon anteriorly and the flexor digitorum longus tendon posteriorly. These tendons may be highlighted by flexing and inverting the foot.

**Special Properties:**
- Confluent point of the Yin Motility vessel
- Luo-connecting point of the Yin Motility vessel
- One of the 'eight therapeutic methods'

# **KID-9** · ZHÚBĪN (築賓) · *Guest House*
## What is the location and special properties of this point?
?
**Location:** On the medial aspect of the lower leg, 5 cun superior to Tàixī KID-3, on the line drawn between Tàixī KID-3 and Yīngǔ KID-10, about 1 cun posterior to the medial border of the tibia.

**Notes:** Locate at the junction of the lower third and upper two thirds of the distance between Tàixī KID-3 and Yīngǔ KID-10. Note that this point is on the same level as Lǐgōu LIV-5.

**Special Properties:**
- Xi-Cleft point of the Yin Linking vessel

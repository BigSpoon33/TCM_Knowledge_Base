# **当归** · Dāng Guī · *Tangkuei, Chinese Angelica Root*
## What are the individual characters and their meanings in this term?
?
**当** (dāng) - ought, bear
  - Radical: ⺌
  - Components: ⺌, 彐

**归** (guī) - return, belong
  - Radical: 丨 (line (vertical line))
  - Components: 丨, 丿, 彐

**Additional Info:** Angelicae Sinensis Radix

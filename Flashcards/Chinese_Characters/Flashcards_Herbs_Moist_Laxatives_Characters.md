# **火麻仁** · Huo Ma Ren · *Hemp Seed*
## What are the individual characters and their meanings in this term?
?
**火** (huǒ) - fire
  - Radical: 人 (person)
  - Components: 人, 八

**麻** (má) - numb
  - Radical: 广 (shelter)
  - Components: 广, 林

**仁** (rén) - benevolence, kernel
  - Radical: 二 (two)
  - Components: 二, 亻

**Additional Info:** Cannabis Semen

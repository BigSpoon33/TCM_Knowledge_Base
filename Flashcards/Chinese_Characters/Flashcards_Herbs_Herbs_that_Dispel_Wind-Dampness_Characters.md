# **威灵仙** · Wei Ling Xian · *Clematis root, Chinese clematis root*
## What are the individual characters and their meanings in this term?
?
**威** (wēi) - power, prestige
  - Radical: 女 (woman)
  - Components: 女, 戌

**灵** (líng) - spirit, soul
  - Radical: 彐 (snout)
  - Components: 彐, 火

**仙** (xiān) - immortal
  - Radical: 亻 (person (radical))
  - Components: 亻, 山

**Additional Info:** Clematidis Radix

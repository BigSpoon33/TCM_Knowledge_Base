# **丁香** · Dīng Xiāng · *Clove*
## What are the individual characters and their meanings in this term?
?
**丁** (dīng) - nail, robust
  - Radical: 一 (one (horizontal line))
  - Components: 一, 亅

**香** (xiāng) - fragrant, aromatic
  - Radical: 日 (sun, day)
  - Components: 日, 禾

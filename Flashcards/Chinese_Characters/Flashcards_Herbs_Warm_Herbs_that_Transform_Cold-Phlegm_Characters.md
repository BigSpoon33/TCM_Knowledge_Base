# **旋复花** · Xuan Fu Hua · *Inula flower*
## What are the individual characters and their meanings in this term?
?
**旋** (xuán) - revolve, return
  - Radical: 方 (square, direction)
  - Components: 方

**复** (fù) - return, repeat
  - Radical: 夂 (go slowly)
  - Components: 夂, 日

**花** (huā) - flower
  - Radical: 化 (transform)
  - Components: 化, 艹

**Additional Info:** Inulae Flos

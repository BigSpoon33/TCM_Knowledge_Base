# 📍 LIV-11 · YĪNLIÁN (陰廉)

**Point Name:** Yin Corner (YĪNLIÁN)
**Channel:** Liver Channel Channel
**Special Properties:** 

---

## 📍 Location

2 cun inferior to Qìchōng ST-30 on the anterior border of m. adductor longus.

**Location Notes:**
i. Qìchōng ST-30 is located on the lower abdomen, 2 cun lateral to the midline, level with the superior border of the pubic symphysis (Qūgǔ REN-2); ii. The tendon of origin of adductor longus arises from the pubic bone and is identified as the most prominent tendon in the groin.

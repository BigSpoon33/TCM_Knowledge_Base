## Etiology & Pathology

*   **Etiology:**  Any imbalance or deficiency
    *   External Pathogenic Factors (Wind, Cold, Heat, Damp) <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
    *   Internal Emotional Factors (Anger, Joy, Worry, Grief, Fear, Shock) <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
    *   Dietary Irregularities <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
    *   Lifestyle factors (Overwork, Lack of Exercise) <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Pathology:** Each factor disrupts the proper flow of Qi and Blood, creating patterns of disharmony. These are analogous to distortions in a standard test pattern.

Note: [The etiology and pathology create the specific distortions or "noise" in the body's energetic system.]

## Differential Diagnosis

Similar patterns may share symptoms.
*  Example: Liver Qi Stagnation vs. Liver Blood Deficiency
    * Liver Qi Stagnation: more characterized by distention and irritability
    * Liver Blood Deficiency: more characterized by dizziness and blurred vision.

Note: [Careful analysis of ALL signs and symptoms is critical.]

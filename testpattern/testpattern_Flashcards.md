# testpattern Flashcards


## testpattern
What is the fundamental principle behind a test pattern?
?
To provide a standardized, observable signal that reveals the performance characteristics of the system under test.

## testpattern
Name three common types of analog television test patterns.
?

## testpattern
What is the purpose of a resolution chart in digital television test patterns?
?
To evaluate the ability of a display to render fine details, measured in lines per picture height (LPPH).

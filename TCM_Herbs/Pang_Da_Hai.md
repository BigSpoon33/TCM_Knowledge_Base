Actions & Indications 
• Clears and disseminates Lung qi: for sore throat, hoarse-
ness, phlegm-heat cough, and constrained Lung qi. Used 
alone as a tea for hoarseness. 
-
With Platycodi Radix (jie geng), Cicadae Periostracum 
(chdn tul), and Fritillariae Bulbus (bei mu) for hoarseness 
due to Lung heat, or cough due to phlegm-heat. 
-
With Scrophulariae Radix (xudn shen) for hoarseness, 
pain, and swelling of the throat due to fire from yin defi-
ciency. 
-
With Acori tatarinowii Rhizoma (shf chang pu) and 
Menthae haplocalycis Herba (bo he) for hoarseness due to 
wind-heat invading the Lungs. 
-
With Glehniae/ Adenophorae Radix ( [bei!ndn} shii shen), 
Rehmanniae Radix (sheng dl huang), and Glycyrrhizae 
Radix (giin cao) for hoarseness due to either Lung heat 
impairing the proper dissemination of Lung qi or overuse 
of the voice depleting the qi and injuring the fluids. 
• Moistens the Intestines and unblocks the bowels: for mild 
to moderate constipation due to heat clumping in the 
Intestines, often with concurrent headache, red eyes, and 
fever. Can be used alone for mild cases. 
-
With Cannabis Semen (huo md ren) and Pruni Semen 
(yu li ren) for constipation due to heat clumping in the 
Intestines. For serious cases, must be accompanied by 
downward-draining herbs. 
• Encourages the expression of rashes: used adjunctively, as 
an external wash, for incomplete expression of rashes.

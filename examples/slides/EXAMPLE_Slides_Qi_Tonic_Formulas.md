## üí° Study Notes

### Formula Structure Mnemonic
**"Eight Treasures = Four Gentlemen + Four Substances"**
- **4 Gentlemen** (Si Jun Zi Tang) = Qi tonics
- **4 Substances** (Si Wu Tang) = Blood tonics
- **8 Treasures** = Perfect balance

### Key Exam Points
1. **Must know**: Si Jun Zi Tang + Si Wu Tang = Ba Zhen Tang
2. **"Eight Treasures"** = 4 qi herbs + 4 blood herbs
3. **Base for**: Shi Quan Da Bu Tang (add Huang Qi + Rou Gui)
4. Pattern: **Simultaneous** qi and blood deficiency

### Clinical Pearl
> "When a patient is 'completely depleted' from illness, surgery, or chronic stress, think Ba Zhen Tang. Results typically seen within 2-4 weeks. Most effective with adequate rest and nutrition."

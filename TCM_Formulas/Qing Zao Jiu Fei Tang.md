## üéØ Clinical Applications

### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Warm dryness injuring lung yin with heat signs
**Key Symptoms**: Dry cough with little or no phlegm, dry mouth and throat, thirst, dry nose, headache, slight fever, irritability, dry skin, constipation
**Tongue**: Red and dry with little coating
**Pulse**: Rapid and thin, or floating and rapid

### Secondary Applications
- Autumn dryness syndrome
- Chronic dry cough from air conditioning
- Post-febrile dry cough
- Bronchitis with lung dryness
- Chronic pharyngitis with dryness
- Environmental dryness exposure

### Modern Clinical Uses
- Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) with dryness
- Interstitial lung disease with dry cough
- Post-viral cough syndrome
- Chronic pharyngitis and laryngitis
- Occupational lung exposure to dry environments
- Chemotherapy-induced lung dryness

## üìù Therapeutic Actions
**Primary**: Clear dryness, moisten lungs, generate fluids, stop cough

**Secondary**: Nourish yin, reduce inflammation, protect lung tissue, improve breathing

## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Qing Zao Jiu Fei" means "clear dryness and rescue the lungs" - addressing warm dryness that injures lung yin and fluids. Classic formula for autumn dryness syndrome where environmental dryness combines with heat to damage lung function. Balances cooling with moistening to restore normal lung physiology.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows anti-inflammatory, antitussive, and lung-protective effects. Shi Gao provides cooling minerals while E Jiao and Mai Men Dong contain compounds that promote tissue healing and moisture retention. Studies demonstrate benefits in chronic cough, COPD, and lung inflammation. The combination helps restore normal lung fluid balance.

### Clinical Experience
Excellent for chronic dry cough and lung dryness conditions. Particularly effective for people exposed to dry environments, air conditioning, or post-viral respiratory symptoms. Often provides relief within 3-5 days for acute dryness. Best results when combined with environmental humidification and adequate hydration.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, add E Jiao at end of cooking (melt in)
**Timing**: Between meals with warm water, can increase fluid intake
**Duration**: 1-2 weeks for acute dryness, 4-8 weeks for chronic conditions

**Pulse**: Deep, weak, and slow

## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Shen Ling Bai Zhu" represents comprehensive spleen qi tonification with dampness resolution. Classic formula for chronic digestive weakness with internal dampness accumulation. The ten-herb combination addresses both the root (spleen qi deficiency) and the manifestation (dampness and diarrhea). Essential for constitutional digestive weakness.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows gastroprotective, anti-inflammatory, and immunomodulating effects. Ren Shen and Bai Zhu provide compounds that enhance digestive enzyme activity and gut barrier function. Dampness-resolving herbs support healthy fluid metabolism and intestinal function. Studies demonstrate benefits in chronic diarrhea, IBS, and digestive recovery.

### Clinical Experience
Excellent for chronic digestive weakness and diarrhea. Particularly effective for people with weak constitution who have difficulty maintaining normal bowel function. Often used long-term for constitutional digestive support. Best results with dietary modifications to support spleen function (avoid cold, raw, greasy foods).

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 9-12g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, traditionally taken as powder
**Timing**: Before meals to support digestion
**Duration**: 4-12 weeks for chronic conditions, often long-term for constitutional support

## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Plum Stone Qi" (Mei He Qi) describes the sensation of having a plum stone stuck in the throat - classic psychosomatic symptom. Formula treats the interaction between emotional stress and physical symptoms through regulating qi and transforming phlegm.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows anxiolytic, anti-depressant, and gastroprotective effects. The combination demonstrates benefits for stress-related conditions and functional disorders. Studies support use in anxiety, depression, and psychosomatic conditions.

### Clinical Experience
Excellent for stress-related physical symptoms, especially throat and chest sensations. Particularly effective for people with anxiety who experience physical manifestations. Often used as foundation formula with spirit-calming additions.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Cook Ban Xia longer to reduce toxicity, add Sheng Jiang
**Timing**: Between meals, can take with warm water
**Duration**: 2-8 weeks for acute symptoms, longer for chronic conditions

## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Bai Du" means "overcome poison" - referring to the formula's ability to eliminate various pathogenic factors that cause chronic skin problems and recurrent infections. Classic approach for people with weak defensive qi who are susceptible to external pathogenic factors. The formula strengthens the body's natural resistance.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows immune-modulating, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects. Jing Jie and Fang Feng contain compounds that enhance immune function and reduce allergic reactions. The combination demonstrates benefits in chronic skin conditions, recurrent infections, and immune dysfunction. Studies support anti-bacterial and anti-viral activity.

### Clinical Experience
Excellent for people who get sick frequently or have chronic skin problems due to weak immunity. Particularly effective for those with environmental sensitivities, chronic eczema, or post-viral fatigue. Often used as constitutional support for people with poor defensive qi. Best results with lifestyle modifications to support immunity.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, can be taken as powder or granules
**Timing**: Between meals, can increase frequency during acute phases
**Duration**: 2-6 months for constitutional support, shorter for acute conditions

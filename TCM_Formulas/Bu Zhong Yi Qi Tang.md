## üéØ Clinical Applications

### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Spleen and stomach qi deficiency with qi sinking
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic fatigue, prolapse of organs (uterus, rectum, bladder), chronic diarrhea, shortness of breath, weak voice, poor appetite, dizziness upon standing
**Tongue**: Pale with thin white coating
**Pulse**: Weak, especially in the middle position, may be thready

### Secondary Applications
- Chronic fatigue syndrome
- Gastroptosis, nephroptosis
- Chronic diarrhea or loose stools
- Uterine prolapse, rectal prolapse
- Myasthenia gravis
- Post-viral fatigue

### Modern Clinical Uses
- Chronic fatigue syndrome
- Gastroptosis and organ prolapse
- Post-surgical recovery
- Immune deficiency conditions
- Depression with qi deficiency
- Chronic digestive disorders

## üìù Therapeutic Actions
**Primary**: Tonify middle jiao qi, raise yang qi, lift the clear

**Secondary**: Strengthen spleen function, support immune system, regulate digestive function

### Classical Variations
- **For uterine prolapse**: Increase Sheng Ma and Chai Hu, add Chai Hu Gan Cao Tang elements
- **For chronic diarrhea**: Add Shan Yao and Lian Zi
- **For severe fatigue**: Increase Huang Qi to 60g, add Wu Wei Zi

### Modern Modifications
- For immune deficiency: Add Ling Zhi and Dong Chong Xia Cao
- For depression: Add Gan Mai Da Zao Tang herbs

## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
Master formula for "middle qi sinking" - the concept that spleen qi becomes too weak to hold organs in place. Represents the perfect balance of tonifying without stagnation due to lifting herbs.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows immune-modulating effects, potential benefits for chronic fatigue, gastroptosis, and post-viral conditions. Huang Qi contains polysaccharides that support immune function.

### Clinical Experience
Particularly effective for patients with chronic fatigue who feel worse after eating (unlike blood deficiency fatigue). Best taken warm, preferably before meals. Results often seen within 2-3 weeks.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Decoction preferred for acute conditions, granules for maintenance
**Timing**: Before meals, when stomach is empty
**Duration**: 4-12 weeks for chronic conditions

### Formula Structure
- **Si Jun Zi Tang portion**: Ren Shen + Bai Zhu + Fu Ling + Gan Cao (tonify qi)
- **Si Wu Tang portion**: Dang Gui + Shu Di Huang + Bai Shao + Chuan Xiong (nourish blood)

## üéØ Clinical Applications

### Primary Pattern & Manifestations
**Pattern**: Simultaneous qi and blood deficiency
**Key Symptoms**: Chronic fatigue, pale complexion, dizziness, palpitations, shortness of breath, poor appetite, loose stools, irregular menstruation, cold hands and feet
**Tongue**: Pale with thin white coating
**Pulse**: Weak and thready, or weak and slow

### Secondary Applications
- Post-illness recovery with severe deficiency
- Postpartum qi and blood deficiency
- Chronic anemia with digestive weakness
- Chronic fatigue syndrome
- Post-surgical recovery
- Elderly constitutional weakness

### Modern Clinical Uses
- Chronic fatigue syndrome
- Post-chemotherapy recovery
- Severe anemia with constitutional weakness
- Post-surgical recovery and wound healing
- Chronic illness recovery
- Geriatric constitutional support

## üìù Therapeutic Actions
**Primary**: Tonify qi and blood, strengthen spleen, nourish liver

**Secondary**: Improve energy, support digestion, regulate menstruation, enhance immunity

### Modern Modifications
- For cancer recovery: Add Ling Zhi and Dong Chong Xia Cao

## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Eight Treasures" represents the perfect balance of qi and blood tonification. Simply combines the two foundation formulas (Si Jun Zi Tang + Si Wu Tang) to address simultaneous deficiency of both vital substances. Essential formula for severe constitutional weakness.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows immunomodulating, hematopoietic, and energy-enhancing effects. The combination of qi-tonifying and blood-nourishing herbs demonstrates synergistic benefits for fatigue, immunity, and overall vitality. Studies show efficacy in post-chemotherapy recovery.

### Clinical Experience
Powerful formula for severe deficiency conditions. Often used for patients who are "completely depleted" from illness, surgery, or chronic stress. Results typically seen within 2-4 weeks. Most effective with adequate rest and nutrition.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 9-12g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, can be taken as pills for long-term use
**Timing**: Between meals for best absorption
**Duration**: 3-6 months for severe deficiency, longer for constitutional support

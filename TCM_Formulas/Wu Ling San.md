## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
Master formula for water metabolism disorders. The combination of draining (Fu Ling, Zhu Ling, Ze Xie) with strengthening (Bai Zhu) and warming (Gui Zhi) creates a balanced approach that resolves dampness without damaging zheng qi.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows diuretic effects, cardioprotective properties, and anti-inflammatory actions. Fu Ling contains triterpenes that affect water metabolism. Ze Xie shows aldosterone-like effects. The formula demonstrates synergistic effects on fluid balance.

### Clinical Experience
Particularly effective for edema that's worse in morning and improves with activity. Often combined with dietary modifications (reduce salt, increase warm foods). Results typically seen within 3-7 days for acute conditions.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or take as powder 6-9g twice daily
**Preparation**: Traditionally taken as powder mixed with warm water
**Timing**: Between meals, avoid taking too late in evening
**Duration**: 1-2 weeks for acute edema, longer for chronic conditions

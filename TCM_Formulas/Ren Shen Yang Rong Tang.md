## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Yang Rong" means "nourish the construction" referring to nourishing the constructive aspect of qi and blood that builds and maintains the body. Comprehensive formula that addresses qi, blood, heart, spleen, and kidney systems. Essential for people with severe constitutional weakness requiring multi-system support.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows adaptogenic, immunomodulating, and energy-enhancing effects. Ren Shen and Huang Qi provide compounds that enhance cellular energy production and immune function. Blood-nourishing herbs support hematopoiesis and circulation. Studies demonstrate benefits in chronic fatigue, immune dysfunction, and post-illness recovery.

### Clinical Experience
Excellent for profound weakness and exhaustion from chronic illness, cancer treatment, or constitutional deficiency. Particularly effective for people who need comprehensive support for multiple organ systems. Often used long-term for constitutional rebuilding. Best results with gradual increase in activity and stress reduction.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 9-12g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, can be taken as pills for long-term use
**Timing**: Morning and evening doses, take with warm water
**Duration**: 3-6 months minimum for constitutional effects, often long-term

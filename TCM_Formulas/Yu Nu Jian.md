## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Jade Lady" represents the cooling, nourishing quality of the formula - like a beautiful lady who is cool and composed. Treats the pattern of "upper heat, lower cold" where stomach fire flares upward due to kidney yin deficiency. Classic formula balancing clearing heat with nourishing yin.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and bone-protecting effects. Shi Gao provides calcium sulfate with cooling properties. Studies demonstrate efficacy in periodontal disease, menopausal symptoms, and inflammatory conditions of the mouth and stomach.

### Clinical Experience
Excellent for dental and oral problems with heat signs. Particularly effective for people with chronic gum problems, recurring mouth ulcers, and heat-related digestive issues. Often used in menopause when hot flashes combine with oral symptoms.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Decoct Shi Gao first for 20-30 minutes, then add other herbs
**Timing**: Between meals to avoid interfering with digestion
**Duration**: 2-6 weeks for acute conditions, longer for chronic patterns

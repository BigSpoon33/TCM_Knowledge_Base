## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Jade Windscreen" refers to the formula's ability to create a protective barrier against pathogenic wind, like a windscreen protects from wind. Classic combination representing the balance between tonification and dispersion - strengthen the interior while protecting the exterior.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows immunomodulating, anti-allergic, and adaptogenic effects. Huang Qi contains polysaccharides that enhance immune function. Studies demonstrate efficacy in reducing frequency of respiratory infections and improving immune markers.

### Clinical Experience
One of the most widely used formulas in modern practice for immune support. Excellent for people who "catch everything" or have frequent respiratory issues. Often used preventively during cold and flu season. Takes 2-4 weeks to build protective effects.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Decoct above doses twice daily, or 6-9g granules twice daily
**Preparation**: Standard decoction, can be taken as powder
**Timing**: Between meals for best absorption
**Duration**: 1-3 months for building immunity, longer for chronic conditions

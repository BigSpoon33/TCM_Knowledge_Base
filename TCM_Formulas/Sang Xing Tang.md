Biomedical Indications

With the appropriate presentation, this formula may be used
to treat a variety of biomedically-defined disorders. These
can be divided into the following groups:

¢ Acute infectious diseases such as the common cold, ma-
laria, hepatitis, meningitis, pulmonary tuberculosis, pneu-
monia, and urinary tract infection

* Digestive problems such as bacillary dysentery, allergic
colitis, chronic hepatitis, cholecystitis, postcholecystectomy

Modifications

¢ For the early stages of malaria, add Dichroae Radix (chang

* For dry coughs from damage to the fluids, add Asparagi
Radix (tidn men dong) and Polygonati odorati Rhizoma
(yt zhi).

* For thirst with edema, take with Five-Ingredient Powder
with Poria (wi ling san).

* For biliary system complaints, add Corydalis Rhizoma (yan
hu sud), Cyperi Rhizoma (xiang fu), and Toosendan Fruc-

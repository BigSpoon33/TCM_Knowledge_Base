## üí° Clinical Notes & Commentary

### Traditional Understanding
"Tian Wang Bu Xin Dan" means "Heavenly Emperor's heart-supplementing elixir" - representing the most comprehensive formula for heart-kidney yin deficiency with spirit disturbance. The 15-herb combination addresses all aspects of yin deficiency affecting the heart and spirit. Classic for severe insomnia and anxiety from constitutional weakness.

### Modern Pharmacology
Research shows sedative, anxiolytic, and neuroprotective effects. The comprehensive herb combination provides multiple compounds that support nervous system function and sleep quality. Studies demonstrate benefits in chronic insomnia, anxiety disorders, and cognitive function. The formula helps restore normal neurotransmitter balance.

### Clinical Experience
Excellent for severe, chronic insomnia and anxiety disorders. Particularly effective for people with constitutional weakness who develop spirit disturbance. Often provides gradual improvement over 4-8 weeks. Best results when combined with stress reduction, good sleep hygiene, and lifestyle modifications to support yin.

### Dosage & Administration
**Standard Dose**: Usually taken as pills/powder, 6-9g twice daily
**Preparation**: Traditionally made into honey pills, modern granules available
**Timing**: Evening dose 1-2 hours before bedtime, morning dose if needed
**Duration**: 8-16 weeks for chronic conditions, gradual reduction as symptoms improve

# Diabetes Flashcards

## Diabetes
What is the TCM name for Diabetes Mellitus and what does it literally mean?
?
*Xiaoke Bing* (消渴病), meaning "Wasting and Thirsting Disease."

**Flashcard 2:**

---
## Diabetes
According to TCM, what is *Xiaoke* considered beyond just a blood sugar imbalance?
?
A systemic disorder of fluid metabolism and organ function arising from imbalances of Yin, Yang, Qi, and Blood.

**Flashcard 3:**

---
## Diabetes
Which three organs are considered pivotal in the TCM understanding of *Xiaoke*?
?
Lungs, Stomach, and Kidneys.

**Flashcard 4:**

---
## Diabetes
How does Lung dryness contribute to symptoms of *Xiaoke*?
?
Lung dryness contributes to symptoms like dry mouth and increased thirst because the Lungs fail to descend and disperse fluids properly.

**Flashcard 5:**

---
## Diabetes
What is the role of the Stomach in *Xiaoke*, and how does it relate to hunger?
?
The Stomach is responsible for receiving, rotting, and ripening food and fluids. Excessive heat in the Stomach leads to increased hunger.

**Flashcard 6:**

---
## Diabetes
Which organ is considered the foundation of Yin and Yang, and why is it important in *Xiaoke*?
?
The Kidneys. Kidney Yin deficiency is a primary underlying factor in *Xiaoke*.

**Flashcard 7:**

---
## Diabetes
What are the "Three Xiao" (三消) and what does each represent?
?
The "Three Xiao" represent the predominant symptom associated with each Jiao (burner/area of the body): Upper Jiao (Thirst), Middle Jiao (Hunger), and Lower Jiao (Urination).

**Flashcard 8:**

---
## Diabetes
List two signs and symptoms associated with Upper Jiao *Xiaoke*?
?
Excessive thirst, dry mouth and throat, drinking copious amounts of fluids (especially cold). Frequent urination (but usually not as profuse as in Lower Jiao). Weight loss. Dry cough. May have a preference for cold drinks.

**Flashcard 9:**

---
## Diabetes
List two signs and symptoms associated with Middle Jiao *Xiaoke*?
?
Excessive hunger, eating a lot but still losing weight. Dry mouth. Constipation. May experience a burning sensation in the stomach. Increased appetite despite weight loss.

**Flashcard 10:**

---
## Diabetes
List two signs and symptoms associated with Lower Jiao *Xiaoke*?
?
Frequent and profuse urination, especially at night. Cloudy urine. Weakness in the lower back and knees. Dizziness and blurred vision. May experience impotence or decreased libido. Dry skin.

## TCM Patterns of Syncope

*   Dampness Obstructing Middle Jiao
*   Phlegm-Heat Disturbance
*   Liver Yang Rising
*   Qi Stagnation

Note: In TCM, syncope is often associated with distinct patterns of imbalance, which we'll examine in detail.

## Dampness Obstructing Middle Jiao

*   **Symptoms:** Dizziness, heavy-headedness, nausea <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Tongue:** Swollen, sticky white coating <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Pulse:** Slippery, soggy <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Clinical Example:** Syncope after fatty meal <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->

Note: This pattern arises from impaired Spleen function and accumulation of dampness, often aggravated by diet and lifestyle.

---

## Phlegm-Heat Disturbance

*   **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of consciousness, possible convulsions, fever <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Tongue:** Red with a sticky yellow coating <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Pulse:** Slippery, rapid <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Clinical Example:** Syncope with high fever & delirium in a patient with history of smoking & alcohol use. <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->

Note: This pattern reflects an excess condition, where heat and phlegm obstruct the orifices of the Heart and disturb the Shen.

---

## Liver Yang Rising

*   **Symptoms:** Headache, dizziness, tinnitus, irritability <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Tongue:** Red, especially on the sides <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Pulse:** Wiry, rapid <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Clinical Example:** Syncope during a heated argument in a hypertensive patient. <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->

Note: This pattern indicates an imbalance where the Liver's Yang energy ascends excessively, often due to underlying Yin deficiency.

---

## Qi Stagnation

*   **Symptoms:** Distending pain, irritability, fluctuating emotions <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Tongue:** Normal or slightly dark <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Pulse:** Wiry <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Clinical Example:** Syncope followed by chest tightness related to stress. <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->

Note: Qi Stagnation is characterized by blocked energy flow, often triggered by emotional stress or constraint.

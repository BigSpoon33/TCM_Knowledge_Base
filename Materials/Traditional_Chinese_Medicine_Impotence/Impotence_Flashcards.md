# Impotence Flashcards

## Impotence
What is the TCM term for impotence (Erectile Dysfunction)?
?
*Yang Wei* (陽痿), which translates to "flaccid yang." It can also be referred to as *Yin Qi Zuo Zhuang* (陰器不用), meaning "the penis is not used".

How does Liver Qi Stagnation contribute to Erectile Dysfunction in TCM?

Emotional factors (stress, anger, frustration) impede the smooth flow of Liver Qi, preventing proper nourishment of the genitals.

## Impotence
What are the main treatment principles for Impotence in TCM?
?
Tonifying Kidney Yang and Jing, Soothing Liver Qi and Promoting its Smooth Flow, Nourishing Heart Blood and Calming the Shen, Clearing Damp-Heat, Transforming Phlegm, and Invigorating Blood and Dispelling Blood Stasis.

What is the primary location of pain in a Jueyin Headache?
?

## jueyin headache
What does Jueyin Headache typically signify in terms of overall health?
?
A deep-seated constitutional imbalance, reflecting a disruption in the harmonious flow of Qi and Blood, particularly affecting the Liver.

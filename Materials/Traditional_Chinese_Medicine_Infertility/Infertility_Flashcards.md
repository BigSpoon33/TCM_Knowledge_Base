# Infertility Flashcards

## Infertility
How does TCM define infertility?
?
Inability to conceive after one year of regular, unprotected intercourse, viewed as a symptom of a systemic imbalance.

---
## Infertility
What are the key diagnostic methods used in TCM to assess infertility?
?
Detailed questioning, pulse diagnosis, tongue diagnosis, and palpation.

## Infertility
What are some common signs and symptoms of Kidney Yin Deficiency in female infertility?
?
Scanty or absent menstruation, hot flashes, night sweats, dry skin, dizziness, tinnitus, lower back pain, infertility.

What is Kidney Jing Deficiency associated with, and what can it lead to?
?
Associated with aging or long-term illness, can lead to long-term infertility, history of multiple miscarriages, developmental delays, and premature aging.

# Infertility Question Bank

### Question 1

**In Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), infertility is primarily viewed as:**

**Correct Answer:** A symptom of a deeper imbalance within the body's energetic systems.

**Explanation:** TCM views infertility as a systemic imbalance, not just a problem of the reproductive organs. It aims to identify the root cause by diagnosing patterns of disharmony.

**Options:**
- A problem solely with the reproductive organs.
- A symptom of a deeper imbalance within the body's energetic systems.
- A condition caused by genetic defects.
- An untreatable condition.

# taiyang headache Flashcards

## taiyang headache
What is a Taiyang headache in TCM, and where is it located?
?
A Taiyang headache in TCM is a headache primarily located in the posterior head and neck region (occiput and nape of the neck), corresponding to the Taiyang meridian pathway.

---
## taiyang headache
Name three sensations that patients may describe when experiencing a Taiyang headache.
?
Throbbing, sharp, dull, stiff, or tight.

## taiyang headache
In TCM, what does a Taiyang headache often indicate regarding external pathogenic factors?
?
It often indicates that external pathogenic factors, such as Wind or Cold, are attacking the Taiyang channel, the outermost defensive layer of the body.

## taiyang headache
What are two common accompanying symptoms of a Taiyang headache?
?
Stiff neck/shoulders and aversion to cold or wind.

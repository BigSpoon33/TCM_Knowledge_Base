## ‚òØÔ∏è Brain Fog: Western vs. TCM

### Western Medicine

*   Descriptive term, *not* a diagnosis.
*   Difficulty concentrating, memory issues, mental fatigue.
*   Causes: Stress, sleep, diet, medical conditions.

### Traditional Chinese Medicine

*   No direct equivalent; *patterns* of disharmony.
*   Impacts *Shen* (Spirit/Mind) and Zang-Fu organs.
*   Focus on root imbalances.

Note: [Highlight the difference between the Western symptom-based approach and the TCM pattern-based approach.]

# Vertigo Flashcards

## Vertigo
How does TCM view Vertigo (眩晕 *xuàn yūn*)?
?
As an imbalance within the body's organ systems, leading to a disturbance of clear *yang* rising to nourish the head, resulting in spinning or unsteadiness.

## Vertigo
What is a common misconception about how TCM treats vertigo?
?
That it solely improves blood circulation to the brain. While circulation is important, TCM addresses underlying organ imbalances.

## Vertigo
In TCM, what do *spinning* vertigo and *lightheadedness* suggest?
?
Spinning vertigo suggests Liver Wind or Phlegm-Fire disturbance. Lightheadedness suggests Qi and Blood Deficiency or Kidney Essence Deficiency.

## 5. Treatment Principles

**Question:**
What are the main treatment principles for Treatment Principles?

**Key Concepts to Cover:**
*   **Strengthening Deficiencies:** Tonifying deficiencies of Qi, Blood, Yin, or Yang, depending on the identified pattern. This enhances the Heart's ability to function properly and calms the *Shen* (spirit).
*   **Regulating Qi Flow:** Moving stagnant Qi, particularly Liver Qi, to ensure smooth flow throughout the body, preventing it from affecting the Heart.
*   **Transforming Phlegm:** Resolving Phlegm accumulation, which can obstruct the Heart's orifices and disturb the *Shen*.
*   **Activating Blood Circulation:** Removing Blood Stasis to improve circulation to the Heart and alleviate pain or discomfort.

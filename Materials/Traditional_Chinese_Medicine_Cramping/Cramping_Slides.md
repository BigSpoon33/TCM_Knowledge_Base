## Clinical Manifestations: Abdominal Cramping

*   **Location:** Lower/Entire Abdomen
*   **Quality:** Sharp, Cramping, Colicky <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Timing:** Before/During Menses, After Eating, Stress <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Aggravating:** Cold, Pressure, Certain Foods, Stress <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Alleviating:** Heat, Massage, Bowel Movement, Relaxation <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Associated Symptoms:** Nausea, Vomiting, Diarrhea, Constipation, Gas, Bloating, Irritability <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->

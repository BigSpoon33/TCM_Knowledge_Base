## Differential Diagnosis
### Wind Heat vs. Wind Cold
| Feature            | Wind Heat                                | Wind Cold                                 |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| **Fever**          | Higher than aversion to cold            | Lower than aversion to cold             |
| **Sore Throat**    | Prominent                              | Mild or absent                            |
| **Nasal Discharge** | Yellow, thick                            | Clear, watery                             |
| **Pulse**          | Superficial, rapid                        | Superficial, tight                        |

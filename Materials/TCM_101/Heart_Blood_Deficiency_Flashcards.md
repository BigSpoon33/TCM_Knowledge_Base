# Heart Blood Deficiency Flashcards

## Heart Blood Deficiency
What is Heart Blood Deficiency (Xin Xue Xu) in TCM?
?
A pattern where the Heart lacks adequate blood nourishment to perform its functions, affecting both physical and mental-emotional well-being.

**Flashcard 2**

---
## Heart Blood Deficiency
What are two key functions of the Heart in TCM that are affected by Heart Blood Deficiency?
?
Circulating blood and housing the *Shen* (mind/spirit).

## Heart Blood Deficiency
How does Spleen Qi Deficiency relate to Heart Blood Deficiency?
?
Spleen Qi Deficiency impairs the production of *Gu Qi* (Food Qi), the foundation of Blood, leading to reduced blood production and potentially Heart Blood Deficiency.

## Heart Blood Deficiency
How does Liver Blood Deficiency relate to Heart Blood Deficiency?
?
Insufficient Liver Blood weakens the Liver's ability to support the Heart and regulate Qi, potentially leading to Heart Blood Deficiency.

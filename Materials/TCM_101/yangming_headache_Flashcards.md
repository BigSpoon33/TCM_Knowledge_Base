# yangming headache Flashcards

## yangming headache
What is the primary location of pain in Yangming Headache?
?
Forehead.

**Flashcard 2**

---
## yangming headache
Which two channels are primarily associated with Yangming Headache in TCM?
?
Stomach (足陽明胃經 – Zú Yángmíng Wèi Jīng) and Large Intestine (手陽明大腸經 – Shǒu Yángmíng Dàcháng Jīng) channels.

**Flashcard 3**

---
## yangming headache
What are some common signs of heat associated with Yangming Headache?
?
Thirst, aversion to heat, constipation, flushed face.

**Flashcard 4**

---
## yangming headache
Name three factors that can aggravate Yangming Headache.
?
Heat exposure, hunger/skipping meals, spicy/greasy foods, stress

---
## yangming headache
What are some characteristics of the tongue in a patient with Yangming Headache?
?
Red or dark red (especially on the front third), yellow, thick coating, possibly swollen.

## yangming headache
What are some characteristics of the pulse in a patient with Yangming Headache?
?
Rapid, forceful, slippery (hua – 滑) or wiry (xian – 弦), especially strong in the right Guan (關) position.

**Flashcard 7**

## yangming headache
How might the pain quality be described in Yangming Headache?
?
Throbbing, distending, burning; dull, heavy ache (if involving Phlegm-Heat).

**Flashcard 8**

---
## yangming headache
According to TCM principles, what functions are primarily affected leading to Yangming Headache?
?
The transformation, transportation, and excretion of food and fluids by the Stomach and Large Intestine channels.

**Flashcard 9**

---
## yangming headache
What time of day might Yangming headaches be worse, and why?
?
Often worse in the afternoon or evening, when Yang energy is at its peak.

**Flashcard 10**

---
## yangming headache
Besides pain relievers, what are some relieving factors for Yangming headache?
?
Rest, cold compress to the forehead, bowel movement (if related to constipation), eating (if related to hunger).

## Clinical Manifestations: Key Signs

*   **Location:** Forehead (band-like)
*   **Quality:** Throbbing, distending, burning (or dull/heavy with Phlegm-Heat)
*   **Timing:** Often worse in afternoon/evening, after eating, skipping meals <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Aggravated by:** Heat, spicy/greasy foods, stress <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->
*   **Relieved by:** Rest, cold compress, bowel movement (if constipated) <!-- .element: class="fragment" -->

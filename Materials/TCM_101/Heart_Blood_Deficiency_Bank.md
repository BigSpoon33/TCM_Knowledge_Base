# Heart Blood Deficiency Question Bank

### Question 1

**In Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), Heart Blood Deficiency is characterized by:**

**Correct Answer:** Insufficient blood nourishing the Heart.

**Explanation:** Heart Blood Deficiency is defined as a state where the Heart lacks adequate blood nourishment to perform its functions effectively. This impacts both the physical body and mental-emotional well-being.

**Options:**
- Excessive blood flow to the heart.
- Insufficient blood nourishing the Heart.
- An imbalance of Qi in the Spleen.
- A blockage of energy in the Liver.

---
### Question 2

**Which of the following organs plays a key role in producing the foundation of Blood, and is related to Heart Blood Deficiency?**

**Correct Answer:** Spleen

**Explanation:** The Spleen is key in producing *Gu Qi* (Food Qi), the foundation of Blood. Spleen Qi Deficiency can lead to Heart Blood Deficiency due to impaired nutrient transformation and transportation.

**Options:**
- Lungs
- Kidneys
- Spleen
- Gallbladder

### Question 3

**Which of the following is a common mental-emotional symptom of Heart Blood Deficiency?**

**Correct Answer:** Anxiety

**Explanation:** Anxiety, restlessness, unease, and apprehension are common mental-emotional symptoms of Heart Blood Deficiency.

**Options:**
- Increased appetite
- Feeling energized and focused
- Anxiety
- Calmness and serenity

---
### Question 4

**Which of the following sleep disturbances is associated with Heart Blood Deficiency?**

**Correct Answer:** Dream-disturbed sleep

**Explanation:** Dream-disturbed sleep, characterized by excessive dreaming and restless sleep with vivid or disturbing dreams, is a symptom of Heart Blood Deficiency, reflecting the *Shen* not being properly anchored by Blood.

**Options:**
- Sleeping too much
- Dream-disturbed sleep
- Sleeping soundly through the night
- Inability to fall asleep at any time

---
### Question 5

**Which of the following is a cardiovascular symptom associated with Heart Blood Deficiency?**

**Correct Answer:** Palpitations

**Explanation:** Palpitations (awareness of heartbeat) are a cardiovascular symptom of Heart Blood Deficiency.

**Options:**
- High blood pressure
- Slow heart rate
- Palpitations
- Increased blood volume

# Heart Blood Deficiency Flashcards

## Heart Blood Deficiency
What is Heart Blood Deficiency (Xin Xue Xu) in TCM?
?
A TCM pattern characterized by insufficient blood nourishing the Heart, affecting both physical and mental-emotional well-being (Shen).

**Flashcard 2**

---
## Heart Blood Deficiency
What are the main functions of the Heart according to TCM that are compromised in Heart Blood Deficiency?
?
The Heart controls blood vessels, circulates blood, and houses the *Shen* (mind/spirit).

**Flashcard 3**

---
## Heart Blood Deficiency
What are two key organs that can contribute to Heart Blood Deficiency and how?
?
Spleen (impaired Gu Qi production) and Liver (insufficient blood storage and smooth flow).

---
## Heart Blood Deficiency
Name three common mental-emotional symptoms of Heart Blood Deficiency.
?
Anxiety, poor memory, difficulty thinking clearly, depression, startled easily, general restlessness.

**Flashcard 5**

## Heart Blood Deficiency
What type of sleep disturbances are associated with Heart Blood Deficiency?
?
Insomnia and dream-disturbed sleep.

## Heart Blood Deficiency
Describe the cardiovascular symptoms often seen in Heart Blood Deficiency.
?
Palpitations, chest discomfort (dull ache or pressure), and shortness of breath upon exertion.

## Heart Blood Deficiency
What are some general symptoms (besides mental/emotional, sleep, and cardiovascular) associated with Heart Blood Deficiency?
?
Dizziness, fatigue, pale complexion, pale lips and nails, poor appetite.

Describe the pulse quality typically associated with Heart Blood Deficiency.

## Heart Blood Deficiency
What dietary factor can contribute to Heart Blood Deficiency?
?

# Lung Yin Deficiency Flashcards

## Lung Yin Deficiency
What are the three fundamental characteristics of Lung Yin Deficiency?
?
Dryness, Deficiency Heat, and Impaired Lung Function.

## Lung Yin Deficiency
How does dryness manifest in Lung Yin Deficiency?
?
Dry mouth and throat, dry skin, dry eyes, and constipation.

## Lung Yin Deficiency
What are some respiratory-specific symptoms of Lung Yin Deficiency?
?
Dry cough (hacking, unproductive, worse at night), scanty phlegm (thick/difficult, possibly blood-streaked), hoarseness, shortness of breath, and a tickling sensation in the throat.

## Lung Yin Deficiency
What is Deficiency Heat in the context of Lung Yin Deficiency, and how does it manifest?
?
It is a "false heat" arising from the relative excess of Yang due to Yin deficiency. It manifests as afternoon/evening fever, night sweats, malar flush, and hot palms/soles.

## Lung Yin Deficiency
How does Lung Yin Deficiency affect the tongue?
?
The tongue is typically red (especially at the tip) with a scanty, absent, or peeled coating. Cracks may be present.

## Lung Yin Deficiency
How does Lung Yin Deficiency affect the pulse?
?
The pulse is typically thin (Xi), rapid (Shu), and possibly floating (Fu). The Lung position (cun) may be weak.

## Lung Yin Deficiency
What other organ systems are commonly affected by Lung Yin Deficiency and how?
?
*   Kidney Yin Deficiency: Chronic Lung Yin Deficiency can deplete Kidney Yin.
*   Liver Qi Stagnation: Dryness/heat can lead to Qi stagnation (irritability, distention).
*   Heart Fire: Insufficient Yin can lead to Heart Yang becoming relatively excessive (insomnia, anxiety).

## Lung Yin Deficiency
What does the "Yin" in Lung Yin Deficiency represent?
?
The moistening, cooling, and nourishing aspect of the Lung.

## Lung Yin Deficiency
Describe the typical cough associated with Lung Yin Deficiency.
?
It's a dry, hacking, and unproductive cough, meaning little or no phlegm is produced. It is often worse at night or in the morning.

## Lung Yin Deficiency
How can the Kidneys affect Lung Yin Deficiency?
?
The Kidneys are the root of Yin and "nourish" the Lungs with fluids. Kidney Yin Deficiency can exacerbate Lung Yin Deficiency, and vice versa.

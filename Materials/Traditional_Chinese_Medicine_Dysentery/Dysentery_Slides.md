## Clinical Manifestations: Damp-Heat

*   **Stool:** Frequent, urgent, bloody/mucusy, burning sensation, offensive odor
*   **Abdomen:** Pain, tenesmus, aggravated by pressure
*   **Systemic:** Possible fever, thirst, irritability, yellow tongue coating, rapid pulse
*   **Etiology:** Contaminated food/water, damp-heat environment, emotional stress
*   **TCM Diagnosis:** Damp-Heat invading Large Intestine, Liver Qi stagnation -> Heat

Note: [Focus on the most common pattern. Clearly outline the key clinical signs and symptoms.]

---

## Clinical Manifestations: Toxic Heat

*   **Stool:** Frequent, forceful, profuse blood/pus, dark red/purplish, extremely foul
*   **Abdomen:** Severe pain, tenesmus, possible fever/delirium
*   **Systemic:** High fever, severe thirst, possible mental confusion, red tongue, forceful pulse
*   **Etiology:** Virulent pathogens, unsanitary conditions, epidemic outbreaks
*   **TCM Diagnosis:** Toxic Heat accumulating in Intestines, Blood Heat

Note: [Describe the acute and severe pattern. Emphasize the intensity of symptoms.]

---

## Clinical Manifestations: Cold-Damp

*   **Stool:** Frequent, white mucus, abdominal pain relieved by warmth, lack of thirst
*   **Abdomen:** Pain, borborygmi, feeling of fullness
*   **Systemic:** Chills, aversion to cold, white greasy tongue, slow soggy pulse
*   **Etiology:** Cold-damp environment, cold/raw foods, pre-existing Yang deficiency
*   **TCM Diagnosis:** Cold-Damp obstructing Large Intestine, Spleen Yang deficiency

Note: [Describe the Cold-Damp pattern. Highlight the aversion to cold and preference for warmth.]

---

## Clinical Manifestations: Deficiency-Cold

*   **Stool:** Intermittent pus/blood, chronic, thin/watery, pain relieved by warmth
*   **Abdomen:** Mild pain/discomfort, preference for warmth, borborygmi
*   **Systemic:** Fatigue, weakness, pale complexion, pale tongue, deep weak pulse
*   **Etiology:** Chronic illness, improper diet, overexertion, emotional stress weakening Spleen/Kidney
*   **TCM Diagnosis:** Spleen/Kidney Yang deficiency, Qi/Blood deficiency

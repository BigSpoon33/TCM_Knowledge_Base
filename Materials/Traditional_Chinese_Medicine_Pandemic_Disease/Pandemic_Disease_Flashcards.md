# Pandemic Disease Flashcards

## Pandemic Disease
In TCM, what are the two main categories that pandemic diseases fall under?
?
*Wen Bing* (溫病) - warm diseases, and *Yi Li* (疫疠) - pestilential diseases.

## Pandemic Disease
Define *Wen Bing* in the context of TCM.
?
Warm disease. TCM category for febrile diseases with rapid onset and progression. They are caused by warm-heat pathogens.

---
## Pandemic Disease
Define *Yi Li* in the context of TCM.
?
Pestilential disease. TCM category for highly contagious and severe epidemic diseases.

## Pandemic Disease
What is *Li Qi* and who first proposed it as a cause of epidemic diseases?
?
*Li Qi* (戾气) is an invisible pestilential factor distinct from the six exogenous evils, proposed by Wu Youke (Wu Youxing) as the primary cause of epidemic diseases.

## Pandemic Disease
Name one key text in TCM history that discusses epidemic diseases and its significance.
?
*Wen Bing Tiao Bian (Systematic Differentiation of Warm Diseases)* by Ye Tianshi. It codified *Wen Bing* theory and emphasized the Wei Qi Ying Xue (Four Levels) and San Jiao (Three Burners) theories.

## Pandemic Disease
Name one key text in TCM history that discusses epidemic diseases and its significance.
?
*Wen Yi Lun (Treatise on Pestilential Diseases)* by Wu Youke. It introduced the concept of *Li Qi*.

## Pandemic Disease
What are the Four Levels (Wei Qi Ying Xue) and why are they important in treating warm diseases?
?
A theory describing the depth of pathogen penetration. It guides treatment from superficial (Wei Qi) to deeper (Xue) levels.

## Pandemic Disease
Describe TCM's general approach to preventing pandemic diseases.
?
Emphasizing lifestyle adjustments (diet, exercise, sleep), avoiding crowds during outbreaks, and using preventative herbal formulas (prophylaxis).

## Pandemic Disease
What is the significance of *Zheng Qi* and *Xie Qi* in the context of pandemic diseases in TCM?
?
Strengthening *Zheng Qi* (Upright Qi) supports the body's ability to fight off the pathogen, while expelling *Xie Qi* (Evil Qi) eliminates the pathogenic factor.

**Flashcard 10:**

## Pandemic Disease
What is Pattern Differentiation and why is it important in TCM treatment of pandemic disease?
?
Identifying the specific pattern of disharmony (e.g., damp-heat, dry-heat) to tailor treatment. Allows for individualized treatment strategies.

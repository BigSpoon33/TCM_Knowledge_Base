# Pandemic Disease Question Bank

### Question 1

**In Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), pandemic diseases primarily fall under which two categories?**

**Correct Answer:** Wen Bing (溫病) and Yi Li (疫疠)

**Explanation:** Pandemic diseases in TCM are categorized as Wen Bing (warm diseases) and Yi Li (pestilential diseases). Wen Bing refers to warm-heat pathogens with rapid progression, while Yi Li encompasses highly contagious and severe epidemic diseases.

**Options:**
- Wind-Cold and Damp-Heat
- Wei Qi and Ying Xue
- Wen Bing (溫病) and Yi Li (疫疠)
- Qi Deficiency and Blood Stasis

### Question 2

**Which historical figure in TCM was the first to propose the concept of *Li Qi* (戾气), an invisible pestilential factor, as the primary cause of epidemic diseases?**

**Correct Answer:** Wu Youke (Wu Youxing)

**Explanation:** Wu Youke was the first to propose the concept of Li Qi, an invisible pestilential factor distinct from the six exogenous evils, as the primary cause of epidemic diseases. This was a revolutionary idea at the time.

**Options:**
- Zhang Zhongjing
- Ye Tianshi
- Wu Youke (Wu Youxing)
- Hua Tuo

### Question 3

**Which of the following is NOT a key aspect of TCM's general approach to managing pandemic diseases?**

**Correct Answer:** Focusing solely on expelling Xie Qi (Evil Qi) without strengthening Zheng Qi (Upright Qi)

**Explanation:** TCM emphasizes a balanced approach that includes both expelling Xie Qi (evil Qi) and strengthening Zheng Qi (upright Qi). Strengthening the body's ability to fight off the pathogen is crucial for recovery and resilience.

**Options:**
- Prevention through lifestyle adjustments and herbal formulas
- Early intervention with prompt diagnosis and treatment
- Pattern differentiation to tailor treatment to specific disharmonies
- Focusing solely on expelling Xie Qi (Evil Qi) without strengthening Zheng Qi (Upright Qi)

### Question 4

**Which text represents the pinnacle of *Wen Bing* theory, emphasizing the Wei Qi Ying Xue (Four Levels) theory and San Jiao (Three Burners) theory for understanding disease progression?**

**Correct Answer:** Wen Bing Tiao Bian (Systematic Differentiation of Warm Diseases)

**Explanation:** Wen Bing Tiao Bian, by Ye Tianshi and elaborated upon by Xue ShengBai, represents the pinnacle of Wen Bing theory. It emphasizes the Wei Qi Ying Xue and San Jiao theories for understanding disease progression.

**Options:**
- Huangdi Neijing (Yellow Emperor's Inner Classic)
- Shang Han Lun (Treatise on Cold Damage)
- Wen Bing Tiao Bian (Systematic Differentiation of Warm Diseases)
- Wen Yi Lun (Treatise on Pestilential Diseases)

---
### Question 5

**Which TCM concept refers to the body's vital or 'upright' energy that must be strengthened to fight off pathogens during a pandemic?**

**Correct Answer:** Zheng Qi

**Explanation:** Zheng Qi (Upright Qi) refers to the body's vital energy. Strengthening Zheng Qi is a key aspect of TCM's approach to managing pandemic diseases, as it supports the body's ability to fight off pathogens.

**Options:**
- Xie Qi
- Li Qi
- Zheng Qi
- Wei Qi

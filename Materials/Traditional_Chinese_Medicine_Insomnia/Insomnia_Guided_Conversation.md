## 3. Etiology & Pathomechanisms

**Question:**
What causes Etiology & Pathomechanisms and how does it develop?

**Key Concepts to Cover:**
**Etiology & Pathomechanisms**
**Primary Etiological Factors in TCM Insomnia:**
*   **Emotional Disturbances:** Prolonged stress, anxiety, worry, overthinking, anger, or grief directly impact the Zang-Fu organs and the flow of Qi, leading to Shen disturbance.
*   **Dietary Irregularities:** Excessive consumption of greasy, spicy, or stimulating foods can generate Damp-Heat or Phlegm-Heat, disrupting the Shen. Irregular eating habits can weaken the Spleen, leading to Blood deficiency and subsequent Heart Blood deficiency.
*   **Overexertion:** Both physical and mental overexertion deplete Qi and Blood, particularly impacting the Heart and Spleen. Insufficient rest prevents the restoration of these vital substances.

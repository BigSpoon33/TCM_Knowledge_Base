# Insomnia Question Bank

### Question 1

**In Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), insomnia is primarily understood as:**

**Correct Answer:** A symptom of deeper imbalances within the body's energetic systems.

**Explanation:** TCM views insomnia as a consequence of root imbalances that disrupt the *Shen* (Spirit/Mind) from being properly housed and anchored, rather than a disease in itself.

**Options:**
- A disease in itself requiring specific medication.
- A temporary condition with no long-term health effects.
- A symptom of deeper imbalances within the body's energetic systems.
- A psychological issue requiring therapy.

---
### Question 2

**Which organ system is most closely linked to the *Shen* (Spirit/Mind) in TCM?**

**Correct Answer:** The Heart (Xin)

**Explanation:** The Heart (*Xin*) governs the *Shen* and plays a central role in mental and emotional well-being, which is crucial for sleep.

**Options:**
- The Spleen (Pi)
- The Liver (Gan)
- The Heart (Xin)
- The Kidney (Shen)

### Question 3

**Which of the following patterns is associated with difficulty falling asleep, according to TCM?**

**Correct Answer:** Liver Qi Stagnation

**Explanation:** Liver Qi stagnation can transform into Fire, which agitates the *Shen* and makes it difficult to fall asleep.

**Options:**
- Heart Blood Deficiency
- Kidney Yin Deficiency
- Liver Qi Stagnation
- Spleen Deficiency

---
### Question 4

**Which deficiency is MOST likely to manifest as waking up frequently during the night, according to TCM?**

**Correct Answer:** Kidney Yin deficiency.

**Explanation:** Waking up frequently during the night, may indicate Heart Blood deficiency or Kidney Yin deficiency.

**Options:**
- Heart Fire
- Liver Blood Deficiency
- Spleen Qi Deficiency
- Kidney Yin deficiency.

---
### Question 5

**Which of the following tongue presentations suggests Blood deficiency in TCM?**

**Correct Answer:** A pale tongue

**Explanation:** A pale tongue is a common sign of Blood deficiency in TCM, indicating insufficient nourishment.

**Options:**
- A red tongue
- A pale tongue
- A tongue with a thick yellow coating
- A tongue with a peeled coating

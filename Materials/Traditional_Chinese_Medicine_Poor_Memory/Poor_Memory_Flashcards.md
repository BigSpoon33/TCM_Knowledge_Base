# Poor Memory Flashcards

## Poor Memory
What is *Jian Wang* in TCM?
?
*Jian Wang* (健忘) is the TCM term for poor memory, representing cognitive impairment reflecting imbalances in the body's organ systems associated with mental function.

**Flashcard 2**

---
## Poor Memory
Name three general manifestations of *Jian Wang*.
?
Memory impairment, concentration difficulties, and mental fatigue.

**Flashcard 3**

---
## Poor Memory
What are the key symptoms of Heart Blood Deficiency (*Xin Xue Xu*) related to *Jian Wang*?
?
Poor memory, difficulty concentrating, and anxiety, often accompanied by palpitations and insomnia.

**Flashcard 4**

## Poor Memory
What tongue presentation is commonly associated with Spleen Qi Deficiency (*Pi Qi Xu*)?
?
Pale, swollen, possibly with teeth marks.

**Flashcard 5**

---
## Poor Memory
What pulse presentation is commonly associated with Kidney Essence Deficiency (*Shen Jing Bu Zu*)?
?
Deep, weak, especially at the rear position.

## Poor Memory
What are the key symptoms of Phlegm Obstructing the Orifices (Tan Mi Xin Qiao) related to *Jian Wang*?
?
Poor memory, mental confusion, and sluggishness, often accompanied by a feeling of heaviness in the head and expectoration of phlegm.

## Poor Memory
Name four diagnostic methods used in TCM to assess *Jian Wang*.
?
Detailed patient history, observation, tongue diagnosis, and pulse diagnosis.

How does an elderly patient with Kidney Essence Deficiency likely present?
?

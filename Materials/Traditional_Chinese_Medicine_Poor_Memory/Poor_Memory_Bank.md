# Poor Memory Question Bank

### Question 1

**Which of the following is a common general manifestation of *Jian Wang* (poor memory) in TCM?**

**Correct Answer:** Mental fatigue

**Explanation:** Mental fatigue, characterized by mental exhaustion even after minimal cognitive activity and a feeling of brain fog, is a common general manifestation of *Jian Wang*.

**Options:**
- Increased appetite
- Enhanced concentration
- Mental fatigue
- Improved sleep quality

---
### Question 2

**According to TCM, which pattern is most likely associated with an elderly patient experiencing forgetfulness, lower back pain, tinnitus, and frequent urination at night?**

**Correct Answer:** Kidney Essence Deficiency

**Explanation:** Kidney Essence Deficiency is characterized by symptoms like poor memory, dizziness, tinnitus, lower back pain, weak knees, and frequent urination, especially at night, which aligns with the patient's symptoms.

**Options:**
- Heart Blood Deficiency
- Kidney Essence Deficiency
- Spleen Qi Deficiency
- Phlegm Obstructing the Orifices

---
### Question 3

**A patient experiencing poor memory, headaches, and a dark complexion might be diagnosed with which TCM pattern?**

**Correct Answer:** Blood Stasis

**Explanation:** Blood Stasis is often associated with symptoms such as poor memory, headaches, a dark complexion, fixed pain, and a history of trauma.

**Options:**
- Liver Qi Stagnation
- Blood Stasis
- Heart Yin Deficiency
- Spleen Qi Deficiency

### Question 4

**Which diagnostic method in TCM involves observing the tongue's color, shape, coating, and texture?**

**Correct Answer:** Tongue Diagnosis

**Explanation:** Tongue Diagnosis is a key diagnostic method in TCM where the practitioner observes the tongue's characteristics to identify imbalances in the Zang-Fu organs.

**Options:**
- Pulse Diagnosis
- Palpation
- Tongue Diagnosis
- Detailed Patient History

### Question 5

**Which TCM pattern is most likely to be associated with palpitations, insomnia, and dream-disturbed sleep, in addition to poor memory?**

**Correct Answer:** Heart Blood Deficiency

**Explanation:** Heart Blood Deficiency is characterized by poor memory, difficulty concentrating, anxiety, palpitations, insomnia, and dream-disturbed sleep.

**Options:**
- Kidney Essence Deficiency
- Spleen Qi Deficiency
- Heart Blood Deficiency
- Phlegm Obstructing the Orifices

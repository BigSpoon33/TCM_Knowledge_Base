## EXTERNAL PATHOGEN

**Symptoms:**
- Fever
- fear of drafts
- headache
- coughing
- sore throat
- swollen, puffy face
- eventually entire body swells
- scanty urination

**Tongue:** Thin white moss

**Pulse:** Tense, superficial

## SP LOSING T&T

**Symptoms:**
- Sallow face
- distended epigastrium
- no appetite
- watery stool
- scanty urine
- all four limbs are cold

**Tongue:** Pale, moist material

**Pulse:** Deep, moderate, or deficient

## KD YANG DEF.

**Symptoms:**
- Whole body is edemic, especially below waist
- lower back is sore and heavy
- scanty urine
- all four limbs are cold
- fear of cold
- no spirit
- dark or bright white face

**Tongue:** Swollen, pale material; white moss

**Pulse:** Weak

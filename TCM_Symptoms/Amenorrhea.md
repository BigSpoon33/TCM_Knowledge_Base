
## 10. GYNECOLOGY - AMENORRHEA

### BLOOD/QI DEF.

**Symptoms:**
- Menstrual blood over a period of time gradually diminishes until there is none, watery leukorrhea, acrid odor, sallow face, fatigue, no spirit, limbs are without strength, vertigo, palpitations

**Tongue:** Pale material; thin white moss

**Pulse:** Deep, thready, or deficient

### QI/BLOOD STAG.

**Symptoms:**
- Amenorrhea seems related to emotional stress, menses suddenly cease, distended breasts and flanks, sallow face, headache

**Tongue:** Darkish or purple material

**Pulse:** String-taut or hesitant

### LV/KD YIN DEF.

**Symptoms:**
- Gradual ceasing of menses, weight loss, patient feels hot, afternoon fever, dry skin, dark face, lower back and legs are weak, vertigo, tinnitus, dry mouth, constipation

**Tongue:** Red or scarlet material; little moss

**Pulse:** Thready, slightly rapid

### DAMP-PHLEGM

**Symptoms:**
- Slight discomfort in lower abdomen, abdomen feels full but soft, much leukorrhea, no taste in mouth, no appetite, distended breasts, condition is usually found in overweight women

**Tongue:** Greasy white moss

**Pulse:** Rolling


## 15. HEMORRHAGING - COUGHING BLOOD

---

## LU W-H + DRY

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Fever, dry mouth, dry nose, sore throat, coughing of phlegm with blood, chest pain

**Tongue:** Red material; yellow moss

**Pulse:** Superficial, rapid

---

## YIN DEF. + HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Dry cough, bloody phlegm, afternoon low fever, no bodily strength, cough is worse at night

**Tongue:** Red material; little moss

**Pulse:** Thready, rapid

## BLOOD STASIS

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Cough, coughing of phlegm with blood or bloody foam, piercing chest pain, heart palpitations

**Tongue:** Darkish material or red spots on tongue

**Pulse:** Intermittent or slow, string-taut, hesitant

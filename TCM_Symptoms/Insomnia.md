
## 17. INSOMNIA

---

## BLOOD/QI DEF.

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Insomnia, palpitations, forgetfulness, lethargy, food seems tasteless, little appetite, pale face

**Tongue:** Pale material

**Pulse:** Thready or deficient

---

## HT AND KD NOT COMMUNICATING

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Insomnia and irritability, palpitations, forgetfulness, night sweats, tinnitus, vertigo, lumbago

**Tongue:** Red material

**Pulse:** Rapid, thready

---

## HT/GB QI DEF.

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Insomnia, awakens easily in fright, much dreaming, timidity

**Tongue:** Pale material

**Pulse:** String-taut, thready

---

## ST DISHARMONY

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Insomnia, distended epigastrium, belching, abdominal discomfort, defecation is not smooth, excessive eating

**Tongue:** Greasy moss

**Pulse:** Rolling

---

## LV/GB FIRE

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Insomnia, headache, vertigo, sore flanks, quickness to anger, bitter taste in mouth

**Tongue:** Red material

**Pulse:** String-taut, strong

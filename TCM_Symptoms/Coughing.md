
## LU YIN DEF.

**Symptoms:**
- Dry cough

- red cheeks
- other Deficient Yin signs

**Tongue:** Red material; little moss

**Pulse:** Thready, rapid

## LU QI DEFICIENCY

**Symptoms:**
- Weak cough usually associated with asthma
- frequent colds
- spontaneous sweating
- other Deficient Qi signs

**Tongue:** Pale, moist material

**Pulse:** Deficient or weak

## LU DAMP-PHLEGM

**Symptoms:**
- Cough with copious white expectoration
- distended chest and epigastrium
- no appetite
- fatigue
- symptoms often occur with Deficient SP signs

**Tongue:** Greasy white moss

**Pulse:** Soft or rolling

- other signs of heat

**Tongue:** Greasy yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rolling, rapid

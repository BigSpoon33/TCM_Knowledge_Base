
## 8. FEVER

### WIND-COLD

**Symptoms:**
- Fever, aversion to cold, headache or bodyache, sometimes coughing, sometimes runny or stuffed nose, no thirst

**Tongue:** Thin white moss

**Pulse:** Superficial, tense or superficial, moderate

### WIND-HEAT

**Symptoms:**
- Fever, slight aversion to cold, headache, thirst, sore throat, sometimes cough with yellow phlegm

**Tongue:** Red tip; slightly yellow moss

**Pulse:** Superficial, rapid

### WIND-COLD + YANG DEF.

**Symptoms:**
- Very slight fever, acute onset, desire for more clothing, desire for sleep, great aversion to cold

**Tongue:** Pale, moist material

**Pulse:** Deficient

### SHAOYANG LEVEL

**Symptoms:**
- Fever and chills are distinct (i.e., not simultaneous), no appetite, nausea

**Tongue:** Thin and white, or yellow/white moss

**Pulse:** String-taut

### YANGMING LEVEL

**Symptoms:**
- Only fever, no aversion to cold, thirst, irritability, red face, perspiration

**Tongue:** Red material; dry yellow moss

**Pulse:** Surging, forceful, or rolling, rapid

### YIN DEFICIENCY

**Symptoms:**
- Chronic low afternoon fever, warm palms and soles, insomnia, night sweats, red cheeks

**Tongue:** Reddish material; thin white moss

**Pulse:** Thready, rapid

### QI DEFICIENCY

**Symptoms:**
- Chronic low fever (especially in morning), spontaneous sweating, fear of draft, fatigue, white, shiny face

**Tongue:** Pale material; thin white moss

**Pulse:** Weak

### BLOOD STASIS

**Symptoms:**
- Chronic low fever, no thirst or thirst but no desire to drink, stabbing, fixed pain, hemorrhage

**Tongue:** Dark purple material; or red pimples

**Pulse:** Hesitant

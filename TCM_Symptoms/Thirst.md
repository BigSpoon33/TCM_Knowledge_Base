
## 20. THIRST

## LU/ST DRY-HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Dry mouth and throat
- Desire to drink
- Hot body
- Perspiration
- Slight fever or no cold
- Aversion to heat
- Full abdomen
- Constipation

**Tongue:** Red material

**Pulse:** Excess, rapid or superficial

---

## YIN DEFICIENCY

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Thirst but little desire to drink
- Warm palm and soles
- Insomnia
- Night sweats

**Tongue:** Reddish material; no moss

**Pulse:** Thready, rapid

---

## SP QI DEF. + DAMP

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Thirst but no desire to drink, or a desire for hot fluids
- Tired limbs
- Heavy head and body
- Loose stools

**Tongue:** Pale material; greasy white moss

**Pulse:** Deficient, soft

---

## KD YANG DEF. + WATER

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Thirst but no desire to drink, or vomiting after drinking
- Aversion to cold
- Fatigue
- Cold limbs

**Tongue:** Pallid, pale material; little moss

**Pulse:** Deficient

---

## DAMP-PHLEGM

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Thirst but no desire to drink, or vomiting after drinking
- Excess saliva
- Distended abdomen
- Difficult, scanty, or incomplete urination

**Tongue:** Greasy white moss

**Pulse:** Rolling

---

## BLOOD STASIS

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Thirst
- Irritability
- Desire to gargle water but no desire to swallow
- Other signs of Blood Stasis

**Tongue:** Darkish, purple material

**Pulse:** Hesitant

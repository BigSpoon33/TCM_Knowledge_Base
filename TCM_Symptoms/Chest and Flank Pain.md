
## 2. CHEST AND FLANK PAIN

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Chest and flanks are painful and sore, cough, asthma, yellow or rust-colored phlegm, fever, chills

**Tongue:** Yellow moss, red tip

**Pulse:** Superficial, rapid

---

## LU DRYNESS

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Hot body, painful chest, cough, little phlegm, thirst

**Tongue:** Red material; dry

**Pulse:** Rapid

---

## PHLEGM

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Flanks are painful and distended, frothy cough with phlegm and saliva, cough aggravates pain, sometimes fever, no thirst

**Tongue:** Greasy white moss

**Pulse:** String-taut, rolling

---

## HT YANG DEF. -> BLOOD STASIS

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Sporadic chest pain, sometimes stabbing pain, fixed pain, pressure in chest

**Tongue:** Darkish material or red spots

**Pulse:** Hesitant, weak

---

## HT YANG DEF. DAMP-PHLEGM

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Chest is distended, sore, and painful, pain connects to shoulder, cough with phlegm, shortness of breath or asthma

**Tongue:** Darkish material or red spots

**Pulse:** Rolling

---

## LV/GB D-H

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Painful flanks, distended epigastrium, feeling of distress in chest, nausea, yellow urine, fever, jaundice

**Tongue:** Pale material

**Pulse:** String-taut, rapid

---

## LV QI STAG.

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Distended/painful flanks, impatience or quickness to anger, emotional stress increases pain, uncomfortable chest, little desire to drink

**Tongue:** Thin white or yellow moss

**Pulse:** String-taut

---

## LV BLOOD STASIS

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Stabbing flank pain in fixed position, palpable mass under ribs

**Tongue:** Purple, darkish material

**Pulse:** String-taut, hesitant

---

## LV/KD YIN DEF.

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Flank has slight dull achiness or soreness, dry mouth, irritability, vertigo, lower back pain

**Tongue:** Red material; little moss

**Pulse:** String-taut, thready

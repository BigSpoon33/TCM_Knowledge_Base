
## 9. GYNECOLOGY - ABNORMAL UTERINE BLEEDING

### BLOOD HEAT

**Symptoms:**
- Heavy bleeding, bright red blood, breasts and flanks are distended, red face, dry mouth, irritability

**Tongue:** Red material; yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rapid, big

### SP UNABLE TO CONTROL BLOOD

**Symptoms:**
- Heavy bleeding, pale, thin blood, puffy face, pale, bright face, no spirit, no appetite, watery stool

**Tongue:** Pale material; thin white moss

**Pulse:** Deficient

### QI/BLOOD STAG.

**Symptoms:**
- Constant slight bleeding or sudden heavy bleeding, distended flanks, distended lower abdomen, purple or dark blood clots, passing of clots reduces discomfort, dark face

**Tongue:** Darkish material

**Pulse:** Deep, string-taut, or hesitant

### LV/KD YIN DEF.

**Symptoms:**
- Menses arrive early, small amount of blood, pale or purple blood, vertigo, tinnitus, spots before eyes, sore back, insomnia, dry mouth

**Tongue:** Red, scarlet, and shrivelled material

**Pulse:** Rapid, thready, perhaps string-taut


## 19. SPASMS, TREMORS, AND CONVULSIONS

## EXTERNAL WIND PATHOGEN

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Headache
- Neck and back tremors
- Sometimes tetany
- Fear of cold
- Whole body ache
- Clear spirit

**Tongue:** Variable

**Pulse:** Superficial, tight

---

## EXTREME HEAT -> LV WIND

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- High fever
- Unclear spirit
- Sometimes coma
- Tremors and spasms in four limbs
- Mouth tightly clenched

**Tongue:** Red material

**Pulse:** String-taut, rapid

---

## BLOOD DEF. -> LV WIND

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Vertigo
- Fatigue
- Spasms
- Tremors
- Palpitations

**Tongue:** Pale material

**Pulse:** Thready

---

## YIN DEF. -> LV WIND

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Long-term disorder injures Yin
- Tremor in limbs
- Warm palms and soles
- Tired spirit

**Tongue:** Red material; little moss

**Pulse:** Thready, rapid

---

## BLOOD STASIS -> LV WIND

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Thin body
- Spasms
- Headache
- Pain in fixed location

**Tongue:** Darkish material, or material shows red and purple spots

**Pulse:** Hesitant, thready

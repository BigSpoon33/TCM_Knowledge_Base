## 3. CONSTIPATION

---

## DRY HEAT

**Symptoms:**
- Constipation
- dry stool
- bad breath
- distended abdomen
- scanty, dark urine

**Tongue:** Red material; thin yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rolling, excess

---

## QI STAGNATION

**Symptoms:**
- Constipation
- distended chest and flanks
- no appetite
- belching
- unproductive desire to defecate

**Tongue:** Thin, greasy moss

**Pulse:** String-taut, deep

---

## BLOOD DEF.

**Symptoms:**
- Constipation
- vertigo
- palpitations
- face, lips, and nails are lusterless

**Tongue:** Pale material

**Pulse:** Thready

---

## COLD OBSTRUCTING

**Symptoms:**
- Constipation
- slight abdominal discomfort
- pressure and warmth relieve pain
- clear, copious urine
- cold limbs

**Tongue:** Pale material

**Pulse:** Deep, slow

---

## QI DEFICIENCY

**Symptoms:**
- Constipation
- fatigue
- spontaneous sweating
- patient becomes more tired after defecation
- stool is not dry

**Tongue:** Pale, swollen material

**Pulse:** Deficient

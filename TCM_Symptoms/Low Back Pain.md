
## 18. LOW BACK PAIN

---

## COLD-DAMP

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Lower back feels cold, bending is difficult, patient desires heating pad, pain becomes severe when weather is cold or damp

**Tongue:** Greasy white moss

**Pulse:** Deep, rolling

---

## DAMP-HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Lower back is sore and feels heavy, scanty, dark urine

**Tongue:** Greasy yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rolling, string-taut

---

## UB DAMP-HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Stabbing back pain, frequent, burning, and painful urination, bitter taste in mouth
- Intermittent, severe back pain, pain connects with groin, frequent, painful urination, often with blood, or urine has stones, difficulty passing urine

**Tongue:** Yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rapid, string-taut

---

## LABORING LUMBAGO

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Lower back is sore after exertion, all four limbs are tired, rest relieves pain, no other unusual signs

**Tongue:** Thin white moss

**Pulse:** Moderate

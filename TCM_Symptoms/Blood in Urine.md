
## 14. HEMORRHAGING - BLOOD IN URINE

---

## UB DAMP-HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Bloody urine, frequent and painful urination, thirst, low back pain

**Tongue:** Red material; greasy yellow moss

**Pulse:** String-taut, rapid, or rolling, rapid

---

## SI FIRE

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Bloody urine, urine feels hot when passed, dark urine, painful urination, irritability, dry mouth, tongue ulceration

**Tongue:** Yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rapid

---

## SP/KD DEF.

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Frequent urination with pale red blood, poor appetite, no spirit, fatigue, sallow yellow face, soreness in lower back, vertigo, tinnitus

**Tongue:** Pale material

**Pulse:** Deficient or weak

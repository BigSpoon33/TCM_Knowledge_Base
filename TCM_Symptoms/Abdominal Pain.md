## 1. ABDOMINAL PAIN

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Sudden, urgent, severe pain, warmth relieves and cold aggravates pain, no thirst, clear urine

**Tongue:** Pale white material

**Pulse:** Deep, tense

## LV/GB D-H

**Symptoms (CAM):**

## ST/LI DAMP-HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Pain in abdomen accompanied by diarrhea with pus or blood, patient feels heavy and worse after defecation, burning anus, fever, scanty, dark urine

## INTESTINAL ABSCESS

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Abdomen (especially lower right quadrant) is sore and painful, abdomen resists touch, right foot wants to curl up, sometimes accompanied by fever, vomiting

**Tongue:** Greasy yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rapid

## UB DAMP-HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Urgent pain in lower abdomen that sometimes connects with lower back, burning urination that is painful and rough, may contain blood or granules

**Tongue:** Yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rapid

## LV QI STAG.

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Distended epigastrium and abdomen, distended flanks, patient resists touch, passing gas relieves pain, condition is emotionally related at times

**Tongue:** Thin moss

**Pulse:** String-taut

## BLOOD STASIS

- Sharp, stabbing abdominal pain that connects to sides, pain is located in fixed position, or a palpable mass

**Pulse:** Hesitant

## DEFICIENT COLD

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Slight, dull abdominal pain, patient desires heat and touching, loose stools, lethargy

**Tongue:** Pale material

**Pulse:** Weak

## FOOD RETENTION

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Distended abdomen, soreness, pain that resists touch, patient is repelled by food, belching of sour, rotten material, eating aggravates pain, diarrhea relieves pain

**Tongue:** Greasy moss

**Pulse:** Rolling or deep, excess

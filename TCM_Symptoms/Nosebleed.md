
## 16. HEMORRHAGING - NOSEBLEED

---

## LU HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Nosebleed, fever, cough, little phlegm, dry mouth

**Tongue:** Red material; yellow moss

**Pulse:** Superficial, rapid

---

## ST FIRE

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Nosebleed, dry mouth, bad breath, irritability, constipation

**Tongue:** Red material; yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rolling, rapid, or surging

---

## LV FIRE

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Nosebleed, vertigo, headache, red eyes, bitter taste in mouth, irritability, quickness to anger

**Tongue:** Red material; yellow moss

**Pulse:** String-taut, rapid

---

## YIN DEF. + HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Nosebleed, vertigo, red eyes, dry throat, tinnitus, irritability, insomnia

**Tongue:** Red material; thin yellow moss

**Pulse:** Thready, rapid, with little strength

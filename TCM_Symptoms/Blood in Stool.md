
## 13. HEMORRHAGING - BLOOD IN STOOL

---

## LI HEAT

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Fresh red blood in stool, blood is passed before stool + heat signs

**Tongue:** Yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rapid

---

## DEFICIENT COLD

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Darkish blood in stool, blood is passed after stool, cold limbs, sallow face, tired spirit

**Tongue:** Pale material; white moss

**Pulse:** Deep, thready

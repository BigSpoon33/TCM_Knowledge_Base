## 22. VOMITING

## WIND-COLD -> ST

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Acute onset of vomiting
- Nausea accompanied by fever and fear of cold
- Head and body ache
- Distended chest and abdomen
- Diarrhea

**Tongue:** White moss

**Pulse:** Tense

---

## SUMMER-HEAT OR WIND-HEAT -> ST

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Acute onset of vomiting
- Nausea accompanied by fever
- Thirst
- Irritability
- Diarrhea

**Tongue:** Red material; yellow moss

**Pulse:** Rapid, string-taut or rapid, soft

---

## ST PHLEGM

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Vomiting
- Chest is uncomfortable
- No desire to drink
- Vertigo

**Tongue:** Greasy white moss

**Pulse:** Rolling

---

## LV -> ST

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Vomiting
- Food matter appears in vomitus
- Sour taste in mouth
- Belching
- Distended chest and flanks
- Condition is often emotionally related

**Tongue:** Thin white moss

**Pulse:** String-taut

---

## HEAT -> LV WIND -> ST

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Projectile vomiting
- High fever
- Headache
- Stiff neck
- Convulsions in limbs
- Tetany

**Tongue:** Red or scarlet material

**Pulse:** String-taut, rapid

---

## ST YIN DEF.

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Occasional vomiting
- Dry mouth
- Hunger but no desire to eat
- Other Deficient Yin signs

**Tongue:** Red material; little moss

**Pulse:** Thready, rapid

---

## SP/ST QI DEF.

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Vomiting especially after even slight overeating
- Unpredictable vomiting that is chronic
- Fatigue
- Watery stools
- Pale, white face

**Tongue:** Pale material; little moss

**Pulse:** Deficient

---

## FOOD RETENTION

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Vomiting of sour, rotten food
- Relief after vomiting
- Abdomen is full, sore, and distended after eating
- Constipation or diarrhea

**Tongue:** Greasy moss

**Pulse:** Rolling, excess

---

## ST WORMS

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Vomiting of worms
- Vomiting of clear fluid, saliva, or yellow-green water after eating
- Sore abdomen
- Discomfort is occasional

**Tongue:** (no entry)

**Pulse:** (no entry)

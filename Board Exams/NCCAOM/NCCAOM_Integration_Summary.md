## Study Strategy by Exam

### Foundations of Oriental Medicine (FOM)

**Focus Areas:**
1. **Clinical Examination** (35% of exam)
   - Master tongue diagnosis ‚Üí Review linked tongue notes
   - Master pulse diagnosis ‚Üí Create comprehensive pulse guide
   - Know Ten Questions ‚Üí Create systematic intake guide

2. **Pattern Differentiation** (45% of exam)
   - Eight Principles ‚Üí Review [[Yin and Yang]], [[Cold and Heat]], etc.
   - Zang Fu ‚Üí Review all organ pattern notes (excellent coverage)
   - Qi/Blood/Fluids ‚Üí Review [[Qi Deficiency]], [[Blood Stasis]], etc.
   - Six Stages/Four Levels ‚Üí **Need to create** comprehensive guides

3. **Treatment Principles** (20% of exam)
   - Know when to tonify, drain, warm, cool, etc.
   - Understand root vs. manifestation
   - **Need to create** treatment principles guide

**Study Materials:**
- ‚úÖ Existing: 336 pattern notes, 18 concept notes
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Need: Tongue guide, Pulse guide, Six Stages, Four Levels, Treatment principles

### Biomedicine (BIO)

**Focus Areas:**
1. **Basic Sciences** (15% of exam)
   - Anatomy, Physiology, Pathophysiology
   - Pharmacology, Microbiology
   - **Use**: Bickley, Merck Manual, Katzung

2. **Clinical Sciences** (55% of exam)
   - Patient assessment
   - Diagnostic procedures
   - Medical conditions
   - **Use**: Appendix C (Medical Conditions) in NCCAOM reference

3. **Patient Management** (30% of exam)
   - Red flags and referrals
   - Safety and infection control
   - **Use**: Anzaldua, Clean Needle Technique Manual

**Study Materials:**
- ‚úÖ Existing: Basic TCM disease notes (31 diseases)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Need: Biomedicine is separate from TCM - use recommended textbooks

### Acupuncture with Point Location (ACP)

**Focus Areas:**
1. **Point Location and Functions** (50% of exam)
   - Know all 361 regular channel points
   - Know common extra points
   - **Use**: Existing 362 point notes ‚úÖ

2. **Treatment Principles and Point Selection** (35% of exam)
   - Point selection strategies
   - Point combinations
   - **Need**: Point combination guide, Point categories reference

3. **Needling Techniques** (15% of exam)
   - Needling methods
   - Moxibustion, cupping, gua sha
   - **Use**: Existing technique notes, **Need**: Comprehensive guides

**Study Materials:**
- ‚úÖ Existing: 362 point notes (excellent coverage!)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Need: Point categories guide, Combination strategies, Technique guides

### Chinese Herbology (CH)

**Focus Areas:**
1. **Materia Medica and Formulas** (60% of exam)
   - Know 267 individual herbs

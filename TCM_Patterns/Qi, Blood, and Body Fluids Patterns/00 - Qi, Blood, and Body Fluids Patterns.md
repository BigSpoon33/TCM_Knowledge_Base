## 2. QI, BLOOD AND BODY FLUIDS DIFFERENTIATION

## QI

---

## QI DEFICIENCY

**Symptoms:**
- Breathlessness
- Weak voice
- Spontaneous sweating
- No appetite
- Loose stools
- Tiredness

**Pulse:** Empty pulse

---

## QI SINKING

**Symptoms:**
- Feeling of bearing down
- Tiredness
- Listlessness
- Mental depression
- Prolapse of organs (stomach, uterus, intestines, anus, vagina or bladder)

**Pulse:** Empty pulse

---

## QI STAGNATION

**Symptoms:**
- Feeling of distension
- Distending pain that moves from place to place
- Abdominal masses that appear and disappear
- Mental depression
- Irritability
- Gloomy feeling
- Frequent mood swings
- Frequent sighing

**Pulse:** String-taut or tight pulse

**Tongue:** Slightly purple tongue

---

# BLOOD

---

## BLOOD DEFICIENCY

**Common Symptoms:**
- Sallow complexion
- Pale lips
- Dizziness
- Poor memory
- Numbness
- Blurred vision
- Insomnia

**Tongue:** Pale and slightly dry
**Pulse:** Hesitant and thready

**Detailed Symptoms by Organ:**

**LV (Liver):**
- Pallor
- Dizziness and vertigo
- Insomnia
- Blurring of vision
- "Floaters in eyes"
- Dryness of the eyes
- Night blindness
- Numbness of the limbs
- Spasm of the tendons
- Cramps
- Scanty menstrual flow or amenorrhea
- Withered and brittle nails
- Dry hair and skin
**Tongue:** Pale
**Pulse:** Thready

**HT (Heart):**
- Palpitations
- Insomnia
- Dream-disturbed sleep
- Dizziness and vertigo
- Poor memory
- Pallor
- Pale lips
- Anxiety
- Tendency to be startled
**Tongue:** Pale, thin, slightly dry
**Pulse:** Thready and weak

**Pathological Development:**

| CAUSE         |     | PATHOLOGY        |     | **EFFECT**     |
| :------------ | :-- | :--------------- | :-- | -------------- |
| SP deficiency | →   | BLOOD DEFICIENCY | →   | LV Yang rising |
| LV deficiency | →   | BLOOD DEFICIENCY | →   | LV Wind        |
| KD deficiency | →   | BLOOD DEFICIENCY | →   | Dryness        |


---

## BLOOD STASIS

**Common Symptoms:**
- Dark complexion
- Purple lips
- Pain which is boring, fixed and stabbing in character
- Abdominal masses that do not not move
- Purple nails
- Bleeding with dark blood and dark clots

**Tongue:** Purple
**Pulse:** String-taut, hesitant

**Detailed Symptoms by Organ:**

**LV (Liver):**
- Purple nails
- A dark face
- Painful periods with dark menstrual blood with dark clots
- Abdominal pain
- Premenstrual pain
**Tongue:** Purple tongue especially on the sides
**Pulse:** Wiry or firm pulse

**HT (Heart):**
- Purple lips
- A stabbing or prickling pain in the chest
- Mental restlessness
**Tongue:** Purple tongue on the sides towards the front, purple and distended veins under the tongue
**Pulse:** Choppy or knotted pulse

**LU (Lung):**
- Feeling of oppression of the chest
- Coughing of dark blood
**Tongue:** Purple on the sides towards the front part, purple and distended veins under the tongue

**ST (Stomach):**
- Epigastric pain
- Vomiting of the blood
- Dark blood in the stools
**Tongue:** Purple in the center

**LI (Large Intestine):**
- Severe abdominal pain
- Dark blood in the stools

**UTERUS:**
- Painful periods
- Premenstrual pain
- Dark menstrual blood with dark clots
- Amenorrhea
- Fixed abdominal masses
**Tongue:** Purple tongue
**Pulse:** Wiry or firm pulse

**Cause of Blood Stasis:**

| CAUSE            | MECHANISM       |     | EFFECT       |
| :--------------- | :-------------- | :-- | :----------- |
| Qi stagnation    | fails to move   | →   | BLOOD STASIS |
| Qi deficiency    | fails to move   | →   | BLOOD STASIS |
| Blood Heat       | condenses Blood | →   | BLOOD STASIS |
| Blood deficiency | does not move   | →   | BLOOD STASIS |
| Interior Cold    | congeals Blood  | →   | BLOOD STASIS |

---

## BLOOD HEAT

**Common Symptoms:**
- Feeling of heat
- Skin disease with red eruptions
- Dry mouth
- Bleeding

**Tongue:** Red
**Pulse:** Rapid

**Detailed Symptoms by Organ:**

**HT (Heart):**
- Anxiety
- Mental restlessness
- mouth ulcers

**LV (Liver):**
- Skin disease characterized by itching, heat and redness

**UTERUS:**
- Excessive blood loss during the periods

**LI (Large Intestine):**
- Blood in the stools

---

## BLOOD LOSS

**Common Symptoms:**
- Epistaxis
- Hematemesis
- Hemoptysis
- Melaena
- Menorrhagia
- Metrorrhagia
- Hematuria

**Diagnostic Criteria:**

| CAUSE          | COLOR                | QUANTITY              |
| :------------- | :------------------- | :-------------------- |
| Blood Heat     | Fresh red or dark    | Heavy loss            |
| Blood stasis   | Very dark with clots | Scanty loss           |
| Qi deficiency  | Pale                 | Heavy loss, prolonged |
| Yin deficiency | Bright-red           | Scanty                |

## QI STAGNATION VS. BLOOD STASIS

### QI STAGNATION

**Symptoms:**
- **Pain/distension:** More distension than pain
- **Location:** Moving pain
- **Character:** Distending pain, feeling of fullness
- **Abdominal masses:** Appearing and disappearing
- **Skin:** Not appearing on skin
- **Face:** May be unchanged

**Tongue:** Normal color or slightly purple
**Pulse:** Only slightly string-taut

---

### BLOOD STASIS

**Symptoms:**
- **Pain/distension:** More pain than distension
- **Location:** Fixed pain
- **Character:** Boring or stabbing pain
- **Abdominal masses:** Fixed
- **Skin:** May manifest with purple blotches or bruises
- **Face:** Dark color or bluish-green

**Tongue:** Definitely purple and possibly with purple spots
**Pulse:** String-taut, hesitant

---

## BODY-FLUIDS PATTERNS

### BODY-FLUID DEFICIENCY

**Causes:**
- Diet (Drying food, irregular eating)
- Yin deficiency
- Heavy loss of Blood
- Profuse sweating
- Profuse diarrhea
- Profuse vomiting
- Severe, chronic Blood deficiency

**General Symptoms:**
- **LU:** Dry skin, mouth, nose, cough, lips, tongue

**Target Organs Symptoms:**
- **ST:** Dry skin and dry cough
- **KD:** Dry tongue with horizontal cracks and a dry mouth but without a desire to drink, or with a desire to drink in small sips
- **LI:** Scanty urination, a dry mouth at night and a dry throat
- Dry stools

---

### EDEMA

**Target Organs and Associated Manifestations:**
- **LU:** Not diffusing and descending fluids -> Upper face and hands
- **SP:** Not transforming and transporting -> Middle abdomen and limbs
- **KD:** Not transforming and excreting fluids -> Lower lower part, legs/ankles

**Pitting Edema:**
- Deficiency of Yang of SP, LU or KD

**Non-Pitting Edema:**
- Qi stagnation or Dampness

---

## PHLEGM PATTERNS

### SUBSTANTIAL PHLEGM VS. NON-SUBSTANTIAL PHLEGM

**Essential Manifestation (General Phlegm):**
- Oppression of the chest, nausea, heaviness, a feeling of muzziness of the head and dizziness
- **Tongue:** Swollen body with Sticky coating
- **Pulse:** Slippery or Wiry

### SUBSTANTIAL PHLEGM

**Symptoms:**
- LU phlegm

### NON-SUBSTANTIAL PHLEGM

**Symptoms:**
- **SKIN:** Lumps under the skin, nerve ganglia swellings, swelling of lymph nodes or thyroid, lipomas
- **CHANNELS:** Numbness
- **HT:** Mental illness, mental confusion, depression, anxiety
- **GB or KD:** Gall Bladder or Kidney stones
- **JOINTS:** Bone deformities in chronic rheumatoid arthritis

## TYPES OF PHLEGM

---

## WIND-PHLEGM

**Symptoms:**
- Dizziness
- Nausea
- Vomiting
- Numbness of the limbs
- Coughing
- Phlegm (a rattling sound in the throat and aphasia)

**Additional Notes:**
- CA Board Formula: Ban Xia Bai Zhu Tian Ma Tang

---

## PHLEGM-HEAT

**Symptoms:**
- Expectoration of yellow-sticky phlegm
- A red face
- Dry mouth and lips
- Restlessness

**Tongue:** red tongue with sticky-yellow coating

**Pulse:** rapid-rolling pulse

**Additional Notes:**
- CA Board Formula: Ding Chuan Tang

---

## COLD-PHLEGM

**Symptoms:**
- Expectoration of white-watery phlegm
- A cold feeling in the limbs and back
- Nausea

**Tongue:** pale tongue with white-wet coating

**Pulse:** deep-rolling-slow pulse

**Additional Notes:**
- CA Board Formula: Su Zi Jiang Qi Tang

---

## DAMP-PHLEGM

**Symptoms:**
- Expectoration of very profuse phlegm which is white and sticky
- No thirst
- No appetite
- A feeling of oppression of chest and epigastrium

**Tongue:** sticky tongue coating

**Pulse:** rolling pulse

**Additional Notes:**
- CA Board Formula: Er Chen Tang

---

## QI-PHLEGM

**Symptoms:**
- Non-substantial feeling of swelling in the throat (but no actual swelling)
- A difficulty in swallowing
- A feeling of oppression of chest and diaphragm

**Additional Notes:**
- CA Board Formula: Ban Xia Hou Po Tang

---

## PHLEGM-FLUIDS

**Symptoms:**
- Substantial phlegm characterized by very watery and thin fluids

**Additional Notes:**
- TAN-YIN chart

## PHLEGM FLUID (ST and Intestines) = Phlegm-Fluid (痰飲, ㄉㄢˊㄧㄣˇ)

**Symptoms (FCM):**
- Abdominal fullness and distension
- Vomiting of watery fluids
- A dry tongue and mouth without a desire to drink
- A splashing sound in the stomach
- A feeling of fullness of the chest
- Loose stools
- Loss of weight

**Tongue:** Swollen tongue with sticky coating

**Pulse:** Deep-Slippery or Wiry pulse

**Key Symptoms:** Gurgling sound

---

## SUSPENDED FLUIDS (Hypochondrium) = Suspended Rheum (懸飲, ㄒㄩㄢˊㄧㄣˇ)

**Symptoms (FCM):**
- Hypochondrial pain that is worse on coughing and breathing
- A feeling of distension of the hypochondrium
- Shortness of breath

**Tongue:** Swollen tongue with a sticky coating

**Pulse:** Deep-Wiry pulse

**Key Symptoms:** Cough, ribcage pain

---

## FLOODING FLUIDS (Limbs) = Spillage Rheum (溢飲, ㄧˋㄧㄣˇ)

**Symptoms (FCM):**
- Feeling of heaviness of the body
- A pain in the muscles
- Absence of sweating
- Lack of desire to drink
- A cough with abundant white sputum

**Tongue:** Swollen tongue with a sticky white coating

**Pulse:** Wiry pulse

**Key Symptoms:** Pain, deep heaviness, puffy swelling

---

## PROPPING FLUIDS (Above diaphragm) = Propping Rheum (支飲, ㄓㄜˊㄧㄣˇ)

**Symptoms (FCM):**
- Cough
- Asthma
- Edema
- A feeling of oppression of the chest
- Dizziness
- Abundant white sputum

**Tongue:** Swollen tongue with a sticky, thick, white coating

**Pulse:** Wiry pulse

**Additional Notes:** All the symptoms are aggravated by exposure to cold.

**Key Symptoms:** Cough, panting, inability to lie flat

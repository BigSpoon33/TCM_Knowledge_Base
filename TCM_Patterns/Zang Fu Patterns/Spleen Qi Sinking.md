### Complete Symptom Picture

**Chief Symptoms:**
- Bearing down sensation in the lower abdomen
- Prolapse of organs (uterus, rectum, stomach, bladder)
- Fatigue and weakness, especially after exertion

**Accompanying Symptoms:**
- Shortness of breath
- Frequent urination, especially with a sensation of incomplete emptying
- Loose stools, often with undigested food
- Poor appetite
- Dizziness and lightheadedness
- Pale complexion
- Depression or emotional lability

**Tongue:** Pale, possibly swollen with teeth marks, thin white coat

**Pulse:** Weak, especially in the middle position

##  Contraindications & Cautions

**Treatment Contraindications:**
- Avoid excessive pungent or draining herbs in early stages
- Cautious use with hypertension

**Cautions:**
- Start with gentle tonification and gradually increase the strength of the herbs as the patient improves.
- Monitor the patient for any signs of adverse reactions, such as digestive upset or dizziness.

**When to Avoid:**
- Avoid using strongly draining herbs in patients who are already weak or deficient.

**Biomedical Understanding:**

## ‚úÖ Clinical Pearls & Experience

### Clinical Tips
- Always assess the patient's dietary habits and lifestyle factors, as these often contribute to Spleen Qi Sinking.
- Consider using moxibustion on DU20 (Baihui) and RN6 (Qihai) to further enhance the raising of Qi.
- Rule out other potential causes of organ prolapse, such as childbirth or heavy lifting.

### Common Mistakes
- Neglecting to address Dampness if it is present, which can hinder the effectiveness of treatment.
- Using overly tonifying herbs without addressing underlying Stagnation, which can lead to further imbalance.

### Treatment Modifications
- **If the patient is also experiencing Blood Deficiency:** Add herbs to tonify the Blood, such as Dang Gui and Bai Shao.
- **If the patient is also experiencing Yin Deficiency:** Add herbs to nourish Yin, such as Sheng Di Huang and Mai Men Dong.

### Success Indicators
- A reduction in the bearing down sensation.
- Improvement in energy levels.
- Resolution of organ prolapse.

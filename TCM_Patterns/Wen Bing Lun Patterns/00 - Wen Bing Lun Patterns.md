# 8. WEN BING LUN DIFFERENTIATION

---

## WEI - Defensive-Qi level

**Symptoms:**
- Fever
- Aversion to cold
- Headache
- Slight sweating
- Runny nose with yellow discharge
- Slight thirst
- Sore throat
- Red and swollen tonsils

**Tongue:** Red sides or tip of the tongue

**Pulse:** Superficial, rapid pulse

---

## QI - Qi level

**Symptoms:**
- High fever
- Aversion to heat
- Cough with expectoration of thin-yellow sputum
- Asthma
- Thirst

**Tongue:** Red tongue with thick-yellow coating

**Pulse:** Rolling, rapid pulse

---

## YING - Nutritive-Qi level

**Symptoms:**
- Fever at night
- Dry mouth with no desire to drink
- Insomnia
- Mental restlessness
- Aphasia
- Spots on skin
- In severe cases coma

**Tongue:** Deep-Red tongue

**Pulse:** Thready, Rapid pulse

---

## XUE - Blood level

**Symptoms:**
- High fever
- Skin eruptions
- Vomiting of blood
- Epistaxis
- Blood in stools
- Blood in urine
- Manic behaviour
- In severe cases convulsions

**Tongue:** Deep-Red tongue without coating

**Pulse:** String-taut, Rapid pulse

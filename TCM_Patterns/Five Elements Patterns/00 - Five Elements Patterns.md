## 5. FIVE ELEMENTS DIFFERENTIATION

---

## WOOD ⇨ FIRE (Not Generating Son)

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Timidity
- A lack of courage
- Indecision
- Palpitations
- Insomnia (in particular, waking up in the early hours of the morning)

**Additional Notes:**
- Zangfu Diagnosis: LV/GB not nourishing HT

---

## WOOD ⇨ EARTH (Overacting on Grandson)

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Hypochondriac and epigastric pain
- A feeling of distension
- Irritability
- Loose stools
- Poor appetite
- A greenish face

**Additional Notes:**
- Zangfu Diagnosis: LV Qi Stagnation ⇨ SP deficiency

---

## WOOD ⇨ METAL (Insulting Grandmother)

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Cough
- Asthma
- A feeling of distension of chest and hypochondrium

**Additional Notes:**
- Zangfu Diagnosis: LV insulting LU

---

## FIRE ⇨ EARTH (Not Generating Son)

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Loose stools
- Chilliness
- Weakness of the limbs

**Additional Notes:**
- Zangfu Diagnosis: Mingmen Fire not warming SP

---

## FIRE ⇨ METAL (Overacting on Grandson)

**Symptoms (CAM):**
- Cough with profuse yellow sputum
- A feeling of heat
- A red face

**Additional Notes:**
- Zangfu Diagnosis: Excess Heat in LU

- Zangfu Diagnosis: HT and KD disharmony

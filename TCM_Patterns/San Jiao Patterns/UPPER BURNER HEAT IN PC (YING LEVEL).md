**vs. [[Heat in the Blood]]:**
- Key difference: Heat in the Blood presents with bleeding symptoms and potentially more pronounced skin manifestations, while Upper Burner Heat in PC focuses on Shen disturbance and deep Yin consumption.

**vs. [[Phlegm Fire Harassing the Heart]]:**
- Key difference: Phlegm Fire involves the presence of phlegm signs, such as a greasy tongue coating and potentially rattling sounds in the throat, which are absent in pure Upper Burner Heat in PC.
